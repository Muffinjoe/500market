About 3M

3M is a multinational conglomerate that develops and manufactures a wide range of products, including adhesives, abrasives, laminates, ceramics, and other materials. The company's key products and services include Post-it Notes, Scotch tape, Thinsulate insulation, and various industrial and consumer goods. 3M's innovative products and technologies play a significant role in various industries, including healthcare, consumer goods, and industrial manufacturing.
