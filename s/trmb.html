About Trimble Inc.

Trimble Inc. is a technology company that provides solutions in positioning, mobility, and connectivity for the global construction, agriculture, and transportation industries. The company's key products and services include geospatial and navigation software, mapping and surveying solutions, and Internet of Things (IoT) technology. Trimble's innovative solutions enable businesses to improve productivity, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making, driving growth and efficiency across various sectors.
