About Assurant

Assurant is a global provider of risk management products and services, offering a range of insurance, warranty, and other related products to consumers and businesses. The company's key products and services include property and casualty insurance, vehicle protection, and extended service contracts, among others. By providing these solutions, Assurant helps individuals and businesses manage risk and protect their assets, making it a significant player in the financial services industry.
