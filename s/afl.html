About Aflac

Aflac is a supplemental insurance company providing financial protection to individuals and families in the event of illness or injury. The company's key products and services include cancer, accident, and life insurance policies, as well as dental and vision insurance plans. Aflac's policies provide financial support to policyholders during difficult times, helping to alleviate the financial burden associated with medical expenses.
