About CBRE Group

CBRE Group is a global real estate services and investment company that provides a range of services including commercial property sales, leasing, and property management, as well as valuation, consulting, and investment management. The company's key products and services include commercial property brokerage, facilities management, and project management, among others. As one of the largest commercial real estate services companies in the world, CBRE plays a significant role in shaping the global commercial real estate market and providing strategic solutions to its clients.
