About Extra Space Storage

Extra Space Storage is a self-storage company that owns and operates over 2,500 storage facilities across the United States. The company provides a range of storage solutions, including climate-controlled and drive-up access units, as well as ancillary services such as moving supplies and truck rentals. As a leading provider of self-storage services, Extra Space Storage plays a critical role in supporting the needs of individuals and businesses requiring temporary or long-term storage solutions.
