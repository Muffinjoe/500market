About Occidental Petroleum

Occidental Petroleum is a multinational energy company that explores, produces, and markets crude oil, natural gas liquids, and natural gas. The company operates in the United States, the Middle East, and other regions, with a focus on unconventional oil and gas production. As one of the largest oil and gas producers in the United States, Occidental Petroleum plays a significant role in meeting global energy demands.
