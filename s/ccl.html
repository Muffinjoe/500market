About Carnival

Carnival Corporation & plc is a global cruise company that operates a fleet of cruise ships offering a range of onboard amenities and itineraries to destinations worldwide. The company's key products and services include cruise vacations, onboard dining and entertainment, and shore excursions. As one of the largest cruise operators in the world, Carnival plays a significant role in the global travel and leisure industry.
