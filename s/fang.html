About Diamondback Energy

Diamondback Energy is an independent oil and natural gas company engaged in the acquisition, development, exploration, and production of oil and natural gas properties in the Permian Basin in West Texas. The company's primary products include crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. As a leading player in the Permian Basin, Diamondback Energy plays a significant role in meeting the world's growing energy demands.
