About Charter Communications

Charter Communications is a leading provider of cable television, high-speed internet, and telephone services in the United States. The company offers a range of products and services, including Spectrum TV, Spectrum Internet, and Spectrum Voice, to residential and commercial customers. As a major player in the US broadband market, Charter Communications plays a critical role in connecting millions of households and businesses to the digital economy.
