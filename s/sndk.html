About Sandisk

Western Digital Corporation, the successor to SanDisk, is a leading global data storage solutions provider. The company offers a range of products and services, including flash memory cards, USB drives, solid-state drives (SSDs), and external hard drives, catering to various industries such as consumer electronics, data centers, and automotive. As a key player in the data storage market, Western Digital's innovative solutions enable efficient data management and storage, driving the growth of emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and the Internet of Things.
