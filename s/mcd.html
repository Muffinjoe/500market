About McDonald's

McDonald's is a fast-food giant that operates a global chain of restaurants serving a variety of burgers, fries, chicken nuggets, salads, and breakfast items. The company's iconic brand is recognized for its convenience, affordability, and quick service, making it a staple in the fast-food industry. As one of the world's largest restaurant chains, McDonald's matters to consumers and investors alike due to its widespread presence and influence on the global food market.
