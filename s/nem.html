About Newmont

Newmont Corporation is a leading gold mining company that operates a portfolio of mines globally, producing gold, silver, copper, zinc, and other metals. The company's key products include gold, copper, and other precious and base metals, with a focus on delivering safe, responsible, and sustainable mining practices. As one of the world's largest gold producers, Newmont plays a significant role in meeting global demand for precious metals.
