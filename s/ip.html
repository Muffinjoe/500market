About International Paper

International Paper is a global producer of pulp, paper, packaging, and cellulose fibers, offering a range of products including printing papers, packaging materials, and tissue products. The company serves various end-markets, including packaging, consumer products, and industrial applications, providing essential materials for a wide range of industries. As a leading manufacturer of paper and packaging products, International Paper plays a critical role in facilitating global commerce and connectivity.
