About Palo Alto Networks

Palo Alto Networks is a cybersecurity company that provides network security systems to enterprises, governments, and service providers worldwide. The company's key products and services include its Next-Generation Firewall, Advanced Endpoint Protection, and Cloud Security offerings, which help protect against cyber threats and data breaches. By providing innovative security solutions, Palo Alto Networks helps organizations safeguard their digital assets and maintain the integrity of their networks.
