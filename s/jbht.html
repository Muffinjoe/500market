About J.B. Hunt

J.B. Hunt Transport Services, Inc. is a leading global logistics company that provides a range of transportation and supply chain services, including intermodal, truckload, less-than-truckload, dedicated, refrigerated, and supply chain services. The company operates a large fleet of trucks, intermodal containers, and trailers, and manages a network of terminals and logistics facilities across North America. As a major player in the logistics industry, J.B. Hunt plays a critical role in facilitating the movement of goods and supplies across the supply chain.
