About Linde plc

Linde plc is a multinational industrial gases company that provides a range of products and services, including atmospheric gases, process gases, and liquefied gases, to various industries such as healthcare, food and beverage, and energy. The company's key products include oxygen, nitrogen, argon, and carbon dioxide, as well as specialty gases and equipment for gas processing and handling. As a leading supplier of industrial gases, Linde plays a critical role in supporting the global economy and enabling the production of essential goods and services.
