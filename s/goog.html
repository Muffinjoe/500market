About Alphabet Inc. (Class C)

Alphabet Inc. is a multinational conglomerate that operates as a holding company for a portfolio of businesses, primarily focused on technology and innovation. Its key subsidiaries include Google, a leading internet search and online advertising platform, as well as other businesses such as Waymo, Verily, and Calico. As a leader in the digital landscape, Alphabet's innovative products and services have transformed the way people access information, communicate, and conduct business.
