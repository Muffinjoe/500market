About Hasbro

Hasbro, Inc. is a global play and entertainment company that designs, produces, and markets a range of toys, games, and consumer products, including iconic brands such as Monopoly, Play-Doh, and Nerf. The company's portfolio also includes entertainment franchises like Magic: The Gathering and Dungeons & Dragons, as well as partnerships with popular franchises like Marvel and Star Wars. As a leading player in the global toy and game industry, Hasbro's products bring joy and imagination to children and adults worldwide.
