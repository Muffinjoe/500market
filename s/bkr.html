About Baker Hughes

Baker Hughes is a leading energy technology company that provides integrated oilfield products, services, and digital solutions to the global energy industry. The company's offerings include drilling and production systems, pressure pumping, and artificial lift systems, as well as digital solutions for energy management and optimization. As a key player in the energy sector, Baker Hughes plays a critical role in meeting the world's growing energy demands while promoting operational efficiency and environmental sustainability.
