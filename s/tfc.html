About Truist Financial

Truist Financial is a bank holding company that provides a range of financial services, including personal and commercial banking, investment, mortgage, and insurance products. The company operates through its subsidiaries, offering deposit, loan, and payment services, as well as wealth management and capital markets capabilities. As one of the largest banks in the US, Truist Financial plays a significant role in facilitating economic growth and financial stability for its customers and communities.
