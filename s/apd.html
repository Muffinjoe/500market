About Air Products

Air Products is a global industrial gases company that supplies a range of products and services, including atmospheric gases, process gases, and equipment, to various industries such as energy, healthcare, and manufacturing. The company's key products include oxygen, nitrogen, argon, and carbon dioxide, as well as equipment for gas processing and handling. By providing essential gases and related technologies, Air Products enables its customers to improve efficiency, productivity, and safety in their operations.
