About NVR, Inc.

NVR, Inc. is a homebuilding and mortgage finance company that designs, constructs, and sells single-family and multi-family homes, as well as commercial buildings. The company's key products and services include homebuilding, mortgage financing, and land development. NVR operates through its subsidiaries, including Ryan Homes, NVHomes, Rymar Homes, Fox Ridge Homes, and others, and is a leading player in the US homebuilding industry.
