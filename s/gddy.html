About GoDaddy

GoDaddy Inc is a technology company providing cloud-based products and services to small businesses, web designers, and individuals. Its key offerings include domain name registration, website hosting, and security solutions, as well as e-commerce platforms and digital marketing tools. As a leading provider of online presence solutions, GoDaddy enables businesses to establish a professional online presence and reach their target audiences.
