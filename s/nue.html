About Nucor

Nucor is a leading steel producer and manufacturer of steel products, offering a diverse range of products including steel bars, steel plates, steel sheets, and steel tubing. The company operates a network of mills, recycling facilities, and distribution centers across the United States and Canada, providing a wide variety of steel products to the construction, automotive, and energy industries. As one of the largest steel producers in the United States, Nucor plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing demand for steel products.
