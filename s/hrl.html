About Hormel Foods

Hormel Foods is a multinational consumer goods company that develops, manufactures, and markets a range of branded food products, including meat, poultry, and processed foods such as SPAM, Skippy peanut butter, and Hormel bacon. The company's portfolio of brands caters to various consumer needs, from convenience foods to natural and organic products. As a leading player in the global packaged food industry, Hormel Foods plays a significant role in meeting the evolving demands of consumers worldwide.
