About Sherwin-Williams

Sherwin-Williams is a leading global paints and coatings company that develops, manufactures, and distributes a wide range of products for the architectural, industrial, and consumer markets. The company's key products include paints, coatings, and related products for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. As a major player in the paints and coatings industry, Sherwin-Williams plays a significant role in shaping the built environment and driving innovation in the field of coatings technology.
