About United Parcel Service

United Parcel Service (UPS) is a global logistics and transportation company providing a range of services including package delivery, freight transportation, and supply chain management. The company's key products and services include ground and air package delivery, freight forwarding, and logistics consulting, enabling businesses to efficiently manage their supply chains and reach customers worldwide. As a leading player in the logistics industry, UPS plays a critical role in facilitating global trade and commerce.
