About Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings

Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings is a global cruise and vacation company that operates a diverse fleet of cruise ships, offering a range of onboard amenities and itineraries to destinations worldwide. The company's key products and services include cruises, land and sea vacations, and other travel-related offerings through its three brands: Norwegian Cruise Line, Oceania Cruises, and Regent Seven Seas Cruises. As a leading player in the cruise industry, Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings provides unique travel experiences to millions of passengers annually, generating significant revenue and economic impact.
