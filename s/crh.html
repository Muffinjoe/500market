About CRH plc

CRH plc is a multinational materials company that manufactures and supplies building materials, including aggregates, cement, and building materials solutions, to the construction industry. The company operates in over 30 countries globally and serves a diverse range of customers, from small builders to large infrastructure projects. As a leading supplier of building materials, CRH plays a critical role in supporting the construction of homes, commercial buildings, and infrastructure projects worldwide.
