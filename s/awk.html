About American Water Works

American Water Works Company, Inc. is a holding company that provides water and wastewater services to approximately 15 million people in the United States and the United Kingdom. The company's key products and services include water and wastewater treatment, distribution, and collection, as well as stormwater management. As a leading provider of essential water infrastructure, American Water Works plays a critical role in ensuring public health and safety through reliable access to clean water and sanitation services.
