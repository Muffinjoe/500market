About ConocoPhillips

ConocoPhillips is a multinational energy corporation that explores, produces, transports, and markets crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. The company operates in the upstream, midstream, and downstream segments of the energy industry, offering a range of products and services including refined products, lubricants, and petrochemicals. As one of the world's largest independent exploration and production companies, ConocoPhillips plays a significant role in meeting global energy demands.
