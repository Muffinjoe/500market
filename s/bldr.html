About Builders FirstSource

Builders FirstSource is a leading supplier of building materials, manufactured products, and value-added services to professional builders, including single-family and multi-family residential and light commercial markets. The company offers a range of products, including lumber, roofing, siding, drywall, millwork, doors, and windows, as well as value-added services such as installation and design assistance. As a key supplier to the residential and light commercial construction industry, Builders FirstSource plays a critical role in meeting the growing demand for housing and commercial construction.
