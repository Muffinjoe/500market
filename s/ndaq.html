About Nasdaq, Inc.

Nasdaq, Inc. operates a global technology and information services company serving capital markets, corporate customers, and the financial community. The company provides trading, clearing, exchange technology, regulatory, compliance, and corporate solutions through its various platforms, including the Nasdaq stock exchange. As a leading provider of financial infrastructure and data, Nasdaq plays a critical role in facilitating global capital markets and supporting economic growth.
