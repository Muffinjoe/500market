About Charles River Laboratories

Charles River Laboratories International, Inc. is a global contract research organization that provides a range of services and products to support the discovery, development, and commercialization of pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and medical devices. The company offers laboratory testing and research model services, as well as discovery and safety assessment services for preclinical and clinical research. By providing high-quality research and testing services, Charles River Laboratories enables the development of life-saving treatments and therapies.
