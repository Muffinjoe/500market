About Qualcomm

Qualcomm designs, manufactures, and markets semiconductor products and wireless communication technologies. The company's key products include mobile processors, modems, and Wi-Fi and Bluetooth chipsets, which are used in a wide range of devices, including smartphones, laptops, and automotive systems. As a leader in the development of 5G and other wireless technologies, Qualcomm plays a critical role in enabling the growth of mobile and connected devices.
