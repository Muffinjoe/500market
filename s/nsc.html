About Norfolk Southern

Norfolk Southern Corporation is a leading transportation provider in North America, operating a network of approximately 21,000 miles of track in 22 states, the District of Columbia, and Ontario, Canada. The company offers a range of services, including rail transportation of coal, intermodal, and merchandise freight, as well as logistics and supply chain management solutions. As a critical component of the nation's supply chain, Norfolk Southern plays a vital role in facilitating the movement of goods and commerce across the United States and Canada.
