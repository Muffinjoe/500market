About Cadence Design Systems

Cadence Design Systems is a leading provider of electronic design automation (EDA) software and intellectual property (IP) solutions, enabling the development of complex integrated circuits and systems-on-chip (SoCs). The company's products and services include digital and mixed-signal design tools, verification software, and IP cores, which are used by semiconductor companies and system designers worldwide. Cadence's solutions play a critical role in the development of advanced technologies, including artificial intelligence, 5G networks, and autonomous vehicles.
