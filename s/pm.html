About Philip Morris International

Philip Morris International Inc is a multinational tobacco company that manufactures and sells a range of tobacco products, including cigarettes, heated tobacco, and oral nicotine products. The company's portfolio includes well-known brands such as Marlboro, L&M, and Parliament, among others. As a leading player in the global tobacco industry, Philip Morris International plays a significant role in shaping the global tobacco landscape.
