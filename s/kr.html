About Kroger

Kroger operates as a retailer, offering a range of products including grocery, pharmacy, and general merchandise through its supermarkets, multi-department stores, and specialty stores. The company's key products and services include private-label brands, digital shopping platforms, and pharmacy services. As one of the largest retailers in the United States, Kroger plays a significant role in the country's food retail market, serving millions of customers nationwide.
