About Aptiv

Aptiv is a global technology company that designs, manufactures, and supplies automotive electrical, electronic, and safety systems to original equipment manufacturers. The company's key products and services include vehicle electrification, autonomous driving, and advanced driver-assistance systems. As a leading supplier to the automotive industry, Aptiv plays a critical role in enabling the development of safer, more connected, and more sustainable vehicles.
