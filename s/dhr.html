About Danaher Corporation

Danaher Corporation is a global healthcare and industrial conglomerate that designs, manufactures, and markets a wide range of products and services. The company operates through three segments: Life Sciences, Diagnostics, and Environmental & Applied Solutions, offering products such as laboratory equipment, medical devices, and water quality testing solutions. As a leading player in the healthcare industry, Danaher's innovative solutions and technologies help improve patient care, advance scientific research, and protect public health.
