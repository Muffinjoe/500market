About Adobe Inc.

Adobe is a multinational software company that provides a range of software solutions for creative professionals, businesses, and enterprises. Its key products and services include Creative Cloud, a suite of creative apps such as Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as Experience Cloud, a platform for customer experience management and digital marketing. As a leader in the digital experience management space, Adobe enables businesses to create, manage, and deliver engaging experiences across various channels and devices.
