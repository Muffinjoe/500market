About T-Mobile US

T-Mobile US, Inc. is a wireless communications provider that offers a range of mobile services, including postpaid and prepaid wireless voice, data, and text services. The company's key products and services include its Magenta and Magenta Plus plans, as well as its Home Internet and TV services. As a leading player in the US wireless market, T-Mobile matters for its impact on the telecommunications industry and its influence on consumer mobility and connectivity options.
