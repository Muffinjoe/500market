About ServiceNow

ServiceNow is a cloud-based software company that provides digital workflows for enterprise organizations, helping them automate and manage various business processes. The company's key products and services include the Now Platform, which offers IT service management, customer service management, and security incident response, among other capabilities. By streamlining operations and improving efficiency, ServiceNow enables businesses to enhance customer experiences and drive growth.
