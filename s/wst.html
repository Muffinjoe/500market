About West Pharmaceutical Services

West Pharmaceutical Services is a global company that designs, manufactures, and supplies innovative, high-quality injectable solutions and other pharmaceutical packaging systems. Its key products include syringe systems, cartridges, and vials, as well as contract manufacturing and testing services for the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries. As a leading provider of critical components and services, West Pharmaceutical Services plays a vital role in ensuring the safe and efficient delivery of life-saving medications.
