About Supermicro

Supermicro is a leading provider of server, storage, and networking solutions for the cloud, datacenter, artificial intelligence, and edge computing markets. The company offers a wide range of products, including motherboards, servers, storage systems, and networking equipment, designed to support high-density, high-performance computing applications. As a key supplier to major cloud and datacenter operators, Supermicro plays a critical role in enabling the growth and development of the global digital infrastructure.
