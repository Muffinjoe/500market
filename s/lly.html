About Lilly (Eli)

Eli Lilly and Company is a multinational pharmaceutical corporation that develops, manufactures, and markets human pharmaceuticals and animal health products. The company's key products include diabetes treatments such as Humalog and Trulicity, cancer medications like Alimta and Cyramza, and mental health treatments like Prozac and Cymbalta. As a leading developer of innovative treatments, Eli Lilly plays a significant role in improving patient outcomes and advancing healthcare globally.
