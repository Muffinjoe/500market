About Las Vegas Sands

Las Vegas Sands is a multinational resort and casino company that develops, owns, and operates integrated resorts in Asia, the United States, and Europe, offering a range of amenities and entertainment options. The company's key products and services include gaming, hospitality, and leisure activities, such as hotel rooms, dining, and entertainment venues. As a leading player in the global gaming industry, Las Vegas Sands provides a unique blend of entertainment, leisure, and economic development opportunities in the markets it operates in.
