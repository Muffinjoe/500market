About Chevron Corporation

Chevron is a multinational energy corporation that explores, produces, and transports crude oil and natural gas, as well as refines and markets petroleum products. The company's key products include gasoline, diesel fuel, jet fuel, lubricants, and other petrochemicals. As one of the world's largest energy companies, Chevron plays a significant role in meeting global energy demands while investing in cleaner energy solutions.
