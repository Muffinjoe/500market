About Salesforce

Salesforce is a cloud-based software company that provides customer relationship management (CRM) solutions to businesses of all sizes. Its key products and services include the Salesforce Customer 360 platform, which offers a suite of cloud-based applications for sales, marketing, customer service, and more, as well as its Einstein AI technology. As a leader in the CRM market, Salesforce helps businesses connect with their customers and drive growth through data-driven insights and personalized experiences.
