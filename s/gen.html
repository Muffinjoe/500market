About Gen Digital

Gen Digital is a global consumer software company that provides digital safety and security solutions to individuals and families. The company's key products and services include Norton antivirus software, LifeLock identity theft protection, and other digital security offerings. By protecting consumers from online threats, Gen Digital plays a critical role in maintaining the integrity of the digital ecosystem and safeguarding personal data.
