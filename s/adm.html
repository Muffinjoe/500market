About Archer Daniels Midland

Archer Daniels Midland Company is a global food processing and commodities trading company that converts oilseeds, grains, and other agricultural commodities into a wide range of products, including food ingredients, animal feed, and biofuels. The company's key products include corn, soybean, and wheat-based products, as well as vegetable oils and sweeteners. As a leading player in the global food and feed industry, ADM plays a critical role in meeting the world's growing demand for food, feed, and fuel.
