About Citizens Financial Group

Citizens Financial Group is a bank holding company that provides a range of financial services to individuals, corporations, and other financial institutions through its retail banking and commercial banking segments. The company offers deposit and loan products, including checking and savings accounts, mortgages, and commercial loans, as well as investment and wealth management services. As a leading regional bank in the United States, Citizens Financial Group plays a significant role in supporting economic growth and financial stability in the communities it serves.
