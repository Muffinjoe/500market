About Take-Two Interactive

Take-Two Interactive is a global video game publisher and developer that designs, develops, publishes, and markets entertainment software worldwide. The company's portfolio includes popular franchises such as Grand Theft Auto, Red Dead, NBA 2K, and Borderlands, among others. Take-Two Interactive is a leading player in the gaming industry, known for its high-quality and engaging games that drive revenue and user engagement.
