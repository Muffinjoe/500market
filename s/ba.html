About Boeing

Boeing is a multinational aerospace and defense corporation that designs, manufactures, and sells commercial airplanes, defense, space, and security systems worldwide. The company's key products include commercial jetliners, military aircraft, satellites, and space exploration systems, serving a diverse customer base across industries. As a leading player in the global aerospace industry, Boeing plays a critical role in shaping the future of transportation, defense, and space exploration.
