About Vistra Corp.

Vistra Corp operates as a wholesale electricity provider in the United States, offering a range of products and services including electricity generation, transmission, and distribution, as well as energy trading and retail electricity sales. The company owns and operates a diverse fleet of power generation assets, including coal, natural gas, and solar facilities. As a leading player in the US electricity market, Vistra Corp plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing energy demands.
