About Camden Property Trust

Camden Property Trust is a multifamily real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, manages, and develops apartment communities across the United States. The company's portfolio consists of over 250 properties with more than 140,000 apartment homes, offering a range of living options to residents. As a leading owner and operator of multifamily properties, Camden Property Trust provides a stable source of income and long-term growth for investors.
