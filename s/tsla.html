About Tesla, Inc.

Tesla, Inc. is a leading electric vehicle (EV) and clean energy company that designs, manufactures, and sells EVs, solar panels, and energy storage products. The company's key products include the Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, and Cybertruck, as well as its solar panels and Powerwall energy storage systems. As a pioneer in the EV industry, Tesla matters for its role in accelerating the global transition to sustainable energy and its influence on the automotive and renewable energy sectors.
