About American Electric Power

American Electric Power is a diversified energy company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity to approximately 5.4 million customers across 11 states in the US. The company's key products and services include electricity generation from coal, natural gas, nuclear, and renewable sources, as well as transmission and distribution services. As one of the largest electric utilities in the US, AEP plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing energy demands while promoting a more sustainable energy future.
