About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. The company's key products include iPhones, Mac computers, iPads, Apple Watches, and AirPods, as well as its ecosystem of software and services, including the App Store, Apple Music, and iCloud. As a leading innovator in the technology industry, Apple plays a significant role in shaping the global digital landscape.
