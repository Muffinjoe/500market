About PayPal

PayPal Holdings, Inc. is a digital payments company that enables individuals and businesses to send and receive payments online. The company's key products and services include PayPal, Venmo, and Braintree, which facilitate online transactions, mobile payments, and cross-border payments. As a leading online payment platform, PayPal plays a significant role in the digital economy, connecting buyers and sellers worldwide.
