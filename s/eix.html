About Edison International

Edison International is a multinational energy holding company that generates and distributes electricity through its subsidiaries, including Southern California Edison. The company's key products and services include electricity generation, transmission, and distribution to residential, commercial, and industrial customers in Southern California. As one of the largest electric utilities in the United States, Edison International plays a critical role in meeting the region's energy needs and supporting economic growth.
