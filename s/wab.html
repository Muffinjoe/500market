About Wabtec

Wabtec Corporation is a leading provider of value-added, technology-based equipment, systems, and services for the global rail and transit industries. The company's portfolio includes locomotives, freight cars, transit equipment, and services such as maintenance, repair, and modernization. Wabtec plays a critical role in enabling the efficient movement of people and goods, supporting the growth of global commerce and transportation infrastructure.
