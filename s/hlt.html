About Hilton Worldwide

Hilton Worldwide is a multinational hospitality company that operates a portfolio of 18 world-class brands, including Hilton Hotels & Resorts, Waldorf Astoria Hotels & Resorts, and Hampton by Hilton, offering a range of accommodations and services to leisure and business travelers. The company's global network of over 6,300 properties across 123 countries provides guests with a consistent and high-quality experience. As a leading player in the hospitality industry, Hilton Worldwide matters to travelers and investors alike, offering a unique combination of brand recognition, operational scale, and growth opportunities.
