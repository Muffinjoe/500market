About EOG Resources

EOG Resources is a leading independent crude oil and natural gas company engaged in exploration, development, production, and marketing of hydrocarbon resources primarily in the United States, Canada, and the Permian Basin. The company's key products include crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids, which are essential components of the global energy mix. As a major player in the energy sector, EOG Resources plays a critical role in meeting the world's increasing demand for energy while also driving innovation and sustainability in the industry.
