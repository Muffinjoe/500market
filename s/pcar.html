About Paccar

Paccar Inc is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and distribution of heavy-duty trucks, light trucks, bus, and winch products. The company's key brands include Kenworth, Peterbilt, and DAF, offering a range of commercial vehicles that cater to various industries and applications. As a major player in the global trucking industry, Paccar's products and services play a critical role in facilitating the movement of goods and people, supporting economic growth and development.
