About BNY Mellon

BNY Mellon is a global financial services company that provides investment management, investment services, and wealth management solutions to institutions, corporations, and high net worth individuals. The company offers a range of products and services, including custody, accounting, and fund administration, as well as asset servicing, securities lending, and investment management. As one of the largest custodian banks in the world, BNY Mellon plays a critical role in facilitating global financial transactions and maintaining the integrity of financial markets.
