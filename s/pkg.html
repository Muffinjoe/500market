About Packaging Corporation of America

Packaging Corporation of America is a leading manufacturer of containerboard and corrugated packaging products, offering a range of packaging solutions for various industries. The company's key products include containerboard, corrugated packaging, and specialty packaging products, serving customers across the food, beverage, and consumer goods sectors. As a leading player in the packaging industry, PCA plays a critical role in facilitating the efficient transportation and storage of goods, supporting the growth of e-commerce and other industries.
