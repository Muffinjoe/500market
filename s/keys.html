About Keysight Technologies

Keysight Technologies is a leading technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets electronic design and test equipment, software, and services for the aerospace, automotive, energy, industrial, communications, and semiconductor industries. The company's products and solutions enable customers to accelerate innovation and improve product quality by streamlining the design and test process. Keysight's solutions are critical to the development and deployment of emerging technologies such as 5G, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT).
