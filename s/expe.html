About Expedia Group

Expedia Group is a leading travel technology company that provides a platform for consumers to research, plan, book, and experience travel. The company's key products and services include Expedia.com, Hotels.com, and Orbitz, as well as its loyalty program, Expedia Rewards. By connecting travelers with a vast selection of travel options, Expedia Group enables individuals to explore the world and discover new experiences, driving growth in the global travel industry.
