About Johnson Controls

Johnson Controls is a multinational conglomerate that designs, manufactures, and installs building management systems, HVAC equipment, and fire suppression systems. The company's products and services help to optimize energy efficiency, safety, and comfort in buildings, serving industries such as healthcare, education, and commercial real estate. As a leading provider of smart and sustainable building solutions, Johnson Controls plays a critical role in reducing energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions globally.
