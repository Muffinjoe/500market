About CrowdStrike

CrowdStrike Holdings is a cloud-delivered endpoint security company that provides a range of cybersecurity solutions to protect against cyber threats. Its key products and services include the Falcon platform, which offers endpoint protection, threat intelligence, and incident response capabilities. By providing real-time threat detection and response, CrowdStrike helps organizations protect their networks, systems, and data from sophisticated cyber attacks.
