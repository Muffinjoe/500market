About Royal Caribbean Group

Royal Caribbean Group is a global cruise vacation company that operates a diverse portfolio of brands, including Royal Caribbean International, Celebrity Cruises, and Silversea Cruises. The company offers a range of onboard amenities and experiences, as well as shore excursions and land-based vacations, catering to diverse customer preferences and demographics. As a leading player in the cruise industry, Royal Caribbean Group matters for its significant economic impact and influence on global travel trends.
