About Microsoft

Microsoft is a multinational technology company that develops, manufactures, licenses, and supports a wide range of software products, services, and devices. The company's key products and services include the Windows operating system, Office productivity software, Azure cloud computing platform, and Xbox gaming console. As one of the world's largest and most influential technology companies, Microsoft plays a significant role in shaping the digital landscape and driving innovation in various industries.
