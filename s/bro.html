About Brown & Brown

Brown & Brown is a leading insurance brokerage firm that provides risk management solutions and insurance products to businesses and individuals. The company offers a range of services, including property and casualty insurance, employee benefits, and surety and fidelity insurance, through its network of agents and brokers. By helping clients manage risk and navigate complex insurance markets, Brown & Brown plays a critical role in supporting the stability and growth of the global economy.
