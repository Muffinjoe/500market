About Snap-on

Snap-on Incorporated is a leading global innovator, designer, manufacturer, and distributor of tools, diagnostics, equipment, and software for professional mechanics and technicians. The company's product portfolio includes hand tools, power tools, diagnostic software, and equipment for automotive repair and maintenance, as well as other industries such as aerospace, agriculture, and construction. Snap-on's products and services enable professionals to work more efficiently and effectively, driving productivity and customer satisfaction in the industries they serve.
