About Visa Inc.

Visa Inc. is a payments technology company that facilitates electronic transactions between merchants, financial institutions, and consumers worldwide. The company's key products and services include payment processing, digital wallets, and card issuance, enabling secure and convenient transactions across various channels. As a leading global payments network, Visa plays a critical role in the digital economy, connecting billions of people and businesses worldwide.
