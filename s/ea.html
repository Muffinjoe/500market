About Electronic Arts

Electronic Arts is a leading global interactive entertainment company that develops, markets, and distributes video games for various platforms, including console systems, personal computers, and mobile devices. The company's key products and services include popular franchises such as FIFA, Madden NFL, The Sims, and Battlefield, as well as its EA Sports and EA Games labels. As a major player in the gaming industry, EA matters for its significant influence on the global gaming market and its ability to create engaging experiences for millions of players worldwide.
