About ExxonMobil

Exxon Mobil is a multinational energy corporation that explores, produces, and refines oil and natural gas, as well as manufactures and markets petroleum products, including gasoline, diesel fuel, and jet fuel. The company operates in over 50 countries and is a leading producer of oil and natural gas, with a significant presence in the refining and petrochemicals sectors. As one of the largest publicly traded oil and gas companies in the world, Exxon Mobil plays a critical role in meeting global energy demand and shaping the future of the energy industry.
