About KKR & Co.

KKR & Co. is a global investment firm that provides alternative asset management and private equity solutions to institutional and individual investors. The company offers a range of products and services, including private equity, credit, and real assets, as well as hedge funds and marketable alternative investments. As one of the largest alternative asset managers in the world, KKR & Co. plays a significant role in shaping the global economy through its investments and strategic partnerships.
