About Datadog

Datadog is a cloud-based monitoring and analytics platform that provides real-time insights into software applications and infrastructure performance. The company's key products and services include its eponymous platform, which offers monitoring, logging, and security capabilities, as well as its Synthetics product for automated end-to-end monitoring. By providing a unified view of application performance, Datadog enables businesses to optimize their technology operations and make data-driven decisions.
