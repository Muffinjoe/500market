About Southern Company

Southern Company is a leading energy provider that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity through its subsidiaries, including Alabama Power, Georgia Power, and Mississippi Power. The company's key products and services include electricity, natural gas, and other energy-related services, serving millions of customers across the southeastern United States. As a major player in the US energy sector, Southern Company plays a critical role in meeting the region's growing energy demands while promoting energy efficiency and sustainability.
