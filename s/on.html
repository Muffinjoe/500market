About ON Semiconductor

ON Semiconductor designs, manufactures, and markets a broad portfolio of semiconductor components and solutions for the automotive, industrial, computing, and consumer electronics markets. The company's product offerings include power management, analog, timing, and discrete components, as well as system-on-chip (SoC) solutions. ON Semiconductor's products enable the development of more efficient, reliable, and connected electronic systems, driving innovation in various industries and applications.
