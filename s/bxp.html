About BXP, Inc.

BXP, Inc., a member of the S&P 500, is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates Class A office properties primarily in the United States. The company's portfolio includes high-quality office buildings in major markets such as Boston, New York City, and Washington, D.C. BXP's properties provide a platform for businesses to house their operations, driving economic growth and innovation in the regions they serve.
