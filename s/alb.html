About Albemarle Corporation

Albemarle Corporation is a global leader in the development, manufacture, and supply of lithium and other specialty chemicals for a range of industries, including energy storage, consumer electronics, and automotive. The company's key products include lithium compounds, bromine, and other specialty chemicals used in lithium-ion batteries, pharmaceuticals, and other applications. As a critical supplier to the growing electric vehicle and renewable energy markets, Albemarle plays a vital role in enabling the transition to a more sustainable energy future.
