About Henry Schein

Henry Schein is a healthcare solutions company that provides products and services to office-based dental and medical professionals, as well as animal health customers. The company offers a range of products, including dental equipment, medical equipment, and pharmaceuticals, as well as practice management software and other business solutions. By helping healthcare professionals deliver quality patient care, Henry Schein plays a critical role in improving health outcomes and advancing the healthcare industry.
