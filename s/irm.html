About Iron Mountain

Iron Mountain Incorporated is a global leader in storage and information management services, providing secure and compliant storage solutions for paper, digital, and physical assets. The company's key products and services include data center and cloud storage, document management, and secure shredding and destruction, catering to a wide range of industries including healthcare, finance, and government. By safeguarding sensitive information and preserving physical assets, Iron Mountain plays a critical role in protecting its clients' data and intellectual property.
