About Church & Dwight

Church & Dwight Co., Inc. is a consumer goods company that manufactures and markets a wide range of household, personal care, and specialty products. The company's portfolio includes well-known brands such as Arm & Hammer, OxiClean, and Trojan, among others. As a leading player in the consumer staples sector, Church & Dwight's products are staples in many households, providing essential cleaning, personal care, and hygiene solutions.
