About Deckers Brands

Deckers Brands is a global designer, marketer, and distributor of innovative footwear and accessories. The company's portfolio includes the UGG, Koolaburra, Hoka One One, Teva, and Sanuk brands, offering a range of products from casual and performance footwear to sandals and boots. As a leading player in the premium casual footwear market, Deckers Brands matters to consumers seeking high-quality, comfortable, and stylish products.
