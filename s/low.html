About Lowe's

Lowe's Companies, Inc. operates as a home improvement retailer, offering a wide range of products and services for the repair, maintenance, and improvement of homes and buildings. The company's product offerings include lumber and building materials, appliances, tools, and home decor items, as well as services such as installation and maintenance. As a leading player in the home improvement market, Lowe's provides essential products and services to homeowners and professionals, driving growth in the U.S. housing market.
