About Ventas

Ventas is a healthcare real estate investment trust that invests in and owns a diversified portfolio of medical office buildings, senior housing, and healthcare facilities. The company's key products and services include leasing properties to healthcare providers and operators, as well as providing financing and other services to its tenants. As a leading player in the healthcare real estate sector, Ventas plays a critical role in supporting the delivery of high-quality patient care and improving healthcare outcomes.
