About Hubbell Incorporated

Hubbell Incorporated is a global supplier of electrical and utility infrastructure solutions, providing a wide range of products and services for the construction, industrial, and utility markets. The company's key products include electrical connectors, lighting and controls, and utility solutions, which are used in various applications such as commercial and industrial construction, renewable energy systems, and smart grid infrastructure. As a leading provider of critical infrastructure solutions, Hubbell Incorporated plays a vital role in supporting the growth and development of the global economy.
