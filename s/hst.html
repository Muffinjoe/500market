About Host Hotels & Resorts

Host Hotels & Resorts is a lodging real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates upscale and luxury hotels in the United States, Europe, and Asia. The company's portfolio includes over 130 properties with more than 59,000 rooms, primarily under management agreements with leading hotel brands. As a major player in the hospitality industry, Host Hotels & Resorts provides investors with a diversified portfolio of high-end hotels, generating stable cash flows and long-term growth potential.
