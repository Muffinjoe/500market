About Consolidated Edison

Consolidated Edison is a utility company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity, as well as distributes natural gas to residential, commercial, and industrial customers in New York City and surrounding areas. The company's key products and services include electricity and natural gas distribution, as well as energy-related services such as energy efficiency and renewable energy solutions. As a major provider of essential energy services, Consolidated Edison plays a critical role in powering the economy and daily life in the New York metropolitan region.
