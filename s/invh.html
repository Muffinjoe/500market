About Invitation Homes

Invitation Homes is a real estate investment trust that acquires, renovates, and manages single-family rental properties in the United States. The company's portfolio consists of over 80,000 properties across 16 markets, offering a range of rental homes to tenants. By providing a stable and secure housing option, Invitation Homes plays a significant role in addressing the nation's affordable housing shortage.
