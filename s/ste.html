About Steris

Steris is a global leader in infection prevention and surgical solutions, providing a comprehensive range of products and services that support healthcare facilities in maintaining a safe and sterile environment. The company's key offerings include sterilization and disinfection systems, surgical instrument processing equipment, and infection prevention solutions, among others. By helping to prevent hospital-acquired infections and improve surgical outcomes, Steris plays a critical role in enhancing patient safety and quality of care.
