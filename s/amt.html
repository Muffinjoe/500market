About American Tower

American Tower is a real estate investment trust that specializes in leasing space on communication towers, data centers, and other infrastructure to wireless carriers and broadcasters. The company's key products and services include tower leasing, data center leasing, and other communications infrastructure solutions. As a leading provider of wireless infrastructure, American Tower plays a critical role in supporting the growth and development of the global telecommunications industry.
