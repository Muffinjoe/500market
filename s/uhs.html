About Universal Health Services

Universal Health Services operates a network of acute care hospitals, behavioral health and addiction treatment facilities, and ambulatory surgery centers across the United States. The company provides a range of healthcare services, including inpatient and outpatient treatment for mental health and substance abuse disorders, as well as surgical services and emergency care. As a leading provider of behavioral health services, Universal Health Services plays a critical role in addressing the growing need for mental health and addiction treatment in the US.
