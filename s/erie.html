About Erie Indemnity

Erie Indemnity is a financial services holding company that provides underwriting, risk management, and other insurance-related services to the Erie Insurance Group. The company offers a range of products, including property and casualty insurance, workers' compensation insurance, and specialty insurance products. As a key partner to Erie Insurance Group, Erie Indemnity plays a vital role in supporting the growth and operations of one of the largest property and casualty insurance groups in the United States.
