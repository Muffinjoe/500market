About Nike, Inc.

Nike, Inc. is a multinational consumer goods company that designs, develops, and sells athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. The company's portfolio includes a range of products, including running shoes, basketball sneakers, and sportswear, as well as its popular Jordan and Converse brands. As a leading global sportswear brand, Nike matters for its significant influence on the athletic apparel market and its commitment to innovation, sustainability, and social responsibility.
