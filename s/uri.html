About United Rentals

United Rentals is a leading equipment rental company that provides a wide range of equipment and services to construction, industrial, and oil and gas customers across North America. The company offers a diverse fleet of equipment, including aerial lifts, cranes, forklifts, and generators, as well as services such as equipment maintenance and repair. By providing access to equipment and expertise, United Rentals helps customers manage their capital and labor costs, enabling them to focus on their core business operations.
