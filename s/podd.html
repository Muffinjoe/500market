About Insulet Corporation

Insulet Corporation is a medical technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets insulin pump systems for people with diabetes. The company's flagship product, the OmniPod, is a tubeless, waterproof insulin pump that provides users with greater flexibility and ease of use. By offering innovative and user-friendly solutions, Insulet helps improve the lives of people with diabetes, enabling them to better manage their condition and achieve better health outcomes.
