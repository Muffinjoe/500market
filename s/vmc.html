About Vulcan Materials Company

Vulcan Materials Company is a leading producer of construction aggregates, including crushed stone, sand, and gravel, as well as asphalt mix and cement. The company supplies essential materials to the construction industry, supporting infrastructure development, housing, and commercial projects. As a major player in the US construction materials market, Vulcan Materials plays a critical role in building the nation's infrastructure and driving economic growth.
