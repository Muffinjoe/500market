About Ulta Beauty

Ulta Beauty is a leading beauty retailer that offers a wide range of products from over 500 brands, including its own in-house labels, as well as salon services such as hair cutting, coloring, and styling. The company operates a chain of stores across the United States and also provides e-commerce services, making it a one-stop-shop for beauty enthusiasts. As a major player in the beauty industry, Ulta Beauty matters to consumers and investors alike due to its strong brand portfolio, loyalty program, and growing online presence.
