About Cencora

Cencora is a pharmaceutical company that develops, manufactures, and commercializes a portfolio of generic and specialty pharmaceutical products, including respiratory and dermatology treatments. The company's product offerings include a range of generic and branded medications, as well as a pipeline of in-development products. As a leading player in the pharmaceutical industry, Cencora plays a critical role in providing affordable and accessible healthcare solutions to patients worldwide.
