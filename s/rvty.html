About Revvity

Revvity is a healthcare company that develops and commercializes digital therapeutics for chronic diseases, focusing on mental health and wellness. The company offers a range of products and services, including its flagship digital therapeutic, Revvity's flagship product, which provides personalized mental health support. By leveraging technology to improve patient outcomes, Revvity aims to revolutionize the treatment of mental health conditions and improve the quality of life for millions of people worldwide.
