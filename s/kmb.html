About Kimberly-Clark

Kimberly-Clark is a multinational consumer goods corporation that develops and manufactures personal care and consumer tissue products. The company's key brands include Huggies diapers, Pull-Ups training pants, Depend adult incontinence products, Kleenex facial tissues, and Scott paper products, among others. As a leading provider of essential household and personal care items, Kimberly-Clark plays a significant role in the daily lives of consumers worldwide.
