About Essex Property Trust

Essex Property Trust is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, operates, and manages multifamily residential properties, primarily in the western United States. The company's portfolio consists of over 250 apartment communities with approximately 60,000 units, offering a range of amenities and services to its residents. As a leading multifamily REIT, Essex Property Trust provides a stable source of rental income and a platform for long-term growth in the residential real estate market.
