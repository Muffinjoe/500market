About Roper Technologies

The company's portfolio includes medical imaging and diagnostics, radio frequency identification (RFID), and industrial automation products and services. Roper Technologies' innovative solutions enable customers to improve efficiency, productivity, and safety, driving growth and profitability in their respective markets.
