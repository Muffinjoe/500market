About PepsiCo

PepsiCo is a multinational food and beverage corporation that manufactures, markets, and distributes a diverse portfolio of consumer products, including carbonated soft drinks, juices, waters, snacks, and other food items. The company's key brands include Pepsi, Gatorade, Tropicana, Lay's, Doritos, and Cheetos, among others. As one of the largest food and beverage companies globally, PepsiCo plays a significant role in shaping the consumer packaged goods industry.
