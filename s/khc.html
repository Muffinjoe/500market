About Kraft Heinz

Kraft Heinz is a multinational food and beverage company that manufactures and distributes a wide range of consumer goods, including condiments, cheese, coffee, infant nutrition, and frozen foods. The company's portfolio includes iconic brands such as Kraft, Heinz, Oscar Mayer, and Planters. As one of the largest food companies in the world, Kraft Heinz plays a significant role in the global food industry, providing essential products to households and businesses worldwide.
