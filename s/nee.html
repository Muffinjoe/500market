About NextEra Energy

NextEra Energy is a leading clean energy company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity through its subsidiaries, including Florida Power & Light Company and Gulf Power Company, serving over 10 million customers. The company operates a diverse portfolio of renewable energy sources, including wind and solar power, and is one of the largest producers of wind and solar energy in the world. As a major player in the transition to a low-carbon economy, NextEra Energy plays a critical role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions and promoting sustainable energy solutions.
