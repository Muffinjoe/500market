About Coterra

Coterra Energy, Inc. is a leading independent oil and natural gas producer in the United States, primarily focused on the exploration, development, and production of oil and natural gas properties in the Permian Basin and other key shale plays. The company operates a diverse portfolio of assets, including oil and natural gas wells, pipelines, and midstream infrastructure, enabling it to efficiently develop and transport its hydrocarbon resources. As a major player in the US energy sector, Coterra Energy plays a critical role in meeting domestic energy demand and contributing to the country's energy security.
