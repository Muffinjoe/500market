About S&P Global

S&P Global is a leading provider of financial information and analytics, offering a range of products and services that help investors, corporations, and governments make informed decisions. The company's key offerings include S&P Dow Jones Indices, S&P Global Market Intelligence, and S&P Global Platts, which provide data, research, and ratings on stocks, bonds, and commodities. By providing critical insights and data, S&P Global helps drive transparency, efficiency, and innovation in the global financial markets.
