About Otis Worldwide

Otis Worldwide Corporation is a leading global manufacturer and maintainer of people-moving products, including elevators, escalators, moving walkways, and related services. The company's products are used in commercial and residential buildings, as well as in transportation and infrastructure applications, serving a wide range of industries and customers. As a key player in the global building infrastructure market, Otis Worldwide plays a critical role in enabling safe and efficient movement of people and goods.
