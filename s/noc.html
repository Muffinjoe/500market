About Northrop Grumman

Northrop Grumman Corporation is a leading global aerospace and defense technology company that designs, develops, and manufactures innovative products and solutions for the U.S. military and other government agencies. The company's portfolio includes aircraft, space systems, cyber solutions, and logistics and modernization services, with key products such as the B-21 Raider stealth bomber and the LITENING advanced targeting pod. As a major player in the defense industry, Northrop Grumman plays a critical role in advancing national security and protecting global interests.
