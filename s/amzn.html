About Amazon

Amazon is a multinational e-commerce company that operates as a consumer technology and e-commerce platform, offering a wide range of products and services, including online retail, cloud computing, advertising, and artificial intelligence. The company's key products and services include its e-commerce platform, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and its digital media offerings, such as Prime Video and Amazon Music. As a leading player in the digital economy, Amazon matters for its significant impact on the retail landscape, its innovative approach to technology, and its influence on consumer behavior.
