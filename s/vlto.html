About Veralto

Veralto is a leading provider of industrial automation and control solutions, offering a range of products and services including programmable logic controllers, human-machine interfaces, and industrial communication systems. The company's products enable customers to improve efficiency, productivity, and safety in various industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and power generation. As a key player in the industrial automation market, Veralto plays a critical role in driving innovation and digital transformation in the global industrial sector.
