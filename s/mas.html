About Masco

Masco Corporation is a leading manufacturer of home improvement and building products, offering a diverse portfolio of branded and private-label products, including faucets, cabinets, and doors. The company's products cater to the needs of homeowners, builders, and professionals in the construction industry, driving growth and innovation in the home improvement market. As a key player in the industry, Masco's products and solutions enhance the quality and functionality of homes and buildings, contributing to the company's position as a leading global manufacturer.
