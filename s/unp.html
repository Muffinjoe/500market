About Union Pacific Corporation

Union Pacific Corporation is a Class I freight railroad operating in the United States, providing rail transportation services to agricultural products, construction materials, consumer goods, and industrial products. The company's key products and services include rail transportation, intermodal transportation, and logistics solutions, connecting major West Coast and Gulf Coast ports to eastern and midwestern markets. As one of the largest railroads in North America, Union Pacific plays a critical role in the nation's supply chain, facilitating the movement of goods and supporting economic growth.
