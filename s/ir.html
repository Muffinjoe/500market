About Ingersoll Rand

Ingersoll Rand is a diversified industrial company that designs, manufactures, and sells industrial equipment, including air compressors, air treatment and gas systems, and power tools. The company's products and services are used in various industries such as construction, oil and gas, and mining, and are also used in the production of food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and other consumer goods. Ingersoll Rand plays a critical role in the global economy by providing essential equipment and services that support the production and distribution of goods and services.
