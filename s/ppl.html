About PPL Corporation

PPL Corporation is a multinational utility company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity to approximately 10 million customers in the United States, the United Kingdom, and Latin America. The company's key products and services include electricity generation, transmission, and distribution, as well as natural gas distribution. As a leading utility provider, PPL plays a critical role in meeting the energy needs of its customers and contributing to the stability of the global energy market.
