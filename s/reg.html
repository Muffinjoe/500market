About Regency Centers

Regency Centers is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, operates, and develops high-quality, shopping centers in the United States. The company's portfolio consists of grocery-anchored and community shopping centers that provide a convenient and accessible retail experience for customers. By offering a platform for retailers to connect with consumers, Regency Centers plays a vital role in supporting local economies and driving economic growth.
