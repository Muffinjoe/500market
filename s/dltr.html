About Dollar Tree

Dollar Tree operates a chain of variety stores offering a range of products at fixed prices of $1 or less, including household essentials, food, personal care items, and seasonal merchandise. The company's stores are located throughout the United States and Canada, providing affordable shopping options to budget-conscious consumers. As a leading dollar store retailer, Dollar Tree plays a significant role in the discount retail market, catering to customers seeking value and convenience.
