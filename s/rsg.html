About Republic Services

Republic Services is a diversified waste management company that provides collection, transfer, disposal, and recycling services to municipal, commercial, and industrial customers. The company offers a range of services, including trash collection, recycling, and landfill operations, serving over 14 million customers across the United States. As a leading provider of waste management solutions, Republic Services plays a critical role in maintaining public health and safety, as well as promoting environmental sustainability.
