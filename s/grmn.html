About Garmin

Garmin Ltd. is a global technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets navigation, communication, and information devices. The company's key products include GPS navigation systems, smartwatches, fitness trackers, and aviation electronics, among others. As a leading provider of innovative navigation and wearable technology solutions, Garmin plays a significant role in the consumer electronics industry.
