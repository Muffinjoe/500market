About CF Industries

CF Industries Holdings, Inc. is a leading global fertilizer and industrial chemicals company that manufactures and distributes nitrogen-based products, including ammonia, urea, and diesel exhaust fluid. The company's products are used in agriculture, as well as in industrial applications such as fuel additives and pharmaceuticals. As a key supplier of nitrogen-based products, CF Industries plays a critical role in meeting the world's growing demand for food and other essential products.
