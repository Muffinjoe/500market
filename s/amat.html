About Applied Materials

Applied Materials is a leading provider of equipment, services, and software to the global semiconductor, display, and solar industries. The company's portfolio includes products for wafer fabrication, etching, deposition, and metrology, as well as services for manufacturing and research. By enabling the production of advanced technologies, Applied Materials plays a critical role in driving innovation and growth in key sectors.
