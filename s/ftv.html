About Fortive

Fortive is a diversified industrial technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets professional and industrial products and services. Its portfolio includes brands such as Altra Industrial Motion, Brooks Automation, and Stryker Instruments, among others, offering solutions in motion control, automation, and life sciences. Fortive's products and services enable customers to improve productivity, efficiency, and safety in various industries, driving growth and innovation across the global economy.
