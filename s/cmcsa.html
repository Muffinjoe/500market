About Comcast

Comcast Corporation is a global media, entertainment, and telecommunications company that provides a range of services, including cable television, high-speed internet, and wireless phone service through its Xfinity brand. The company also owns a significant stake in NBCUniversal, a leading media and entertainment company that operates a portfolio of television networks, film studios, and theme parks. As one of the largest media and telecommunications companies in the world, Comcast plays a critical role in shaping the modern media landscape and connecting millions of consumers to their favorite content and services.
