About Parker Hannifin

Parker Hannifin Corporation is a global diversified industrial company that designs, manufactures, and markets motion and control technologies and systems for various mobile, industrial, and aerospace markets. The company's key products and services include hydraulic, pneumatic, and electromechanical systems, as well as filtration and fluid power systems. As a leading provider of critical components and systems, Parker Hannifin plays a vital role in the development and operation of complex equipment and vehicles across various industries.
