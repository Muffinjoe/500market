About Ralph Lauren Corporation

Ralph Lauren Corporation designs, manufactures, and markets lifestyle products, including clothing, accessories, home furnishings, and fragrances, under the Ralph Lauren, Polo, and other brands. The company's products are sold through a network of retail stores, e-commerce sites, and third-party wholesale customers worldwide. As a leading global luxury lifestyle brand, Ralph Lauren Corporation offers high-quality, stylish products that cater to consumers seeking premium fashion and lifestyle experiences.
