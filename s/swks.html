About Skyworks Solutions

Skyworks Solutions is a leading global provider of analog and mixed-signal semiconductors, offering a portfolio of solutions that enable connectivity, mobility, and power management in a wide range of applications, including smartphones, tablets, and automotive systems. The company's products include amplifiers, attenuators, and switches, as well as power management and RF solutions. As a key supplier to major technology companies, Skyworks plays a critical role in the development of next-generation wireless and automotive technologies.
