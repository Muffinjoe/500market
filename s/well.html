About Welltower

Welltower is a healthcare real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests in and manages a diversified portfolio of high-quality, healthcare-related properties, including medical offices, outpatient facilities, and senior housing communities. The company's properties are leased to a diverse group of healthcare providers, including physicians, hospitals, and healthcare systems, providing them with the space and infrastructure needed to deliver quality patient care. By investing in the healthcare real estate sector, Welltower aims to provide stable and growing income to its shareholders.
