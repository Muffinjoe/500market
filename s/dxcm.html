About Dexcom

Dexcom is a medical technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets continuous glucose monitoring systems for people with diabetes. The company's key products include its G5 Mobile and G6 systems, which provide real-time glucose readings and alerts to help manage the condition. By offering accurate and user-friendly glucose monitoring solutions, Dexcom helps improve the lives of people with diabetes and enables them to make informed decisions about their health.
