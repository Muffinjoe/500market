About Synopsys

Synopsys is a leading provider of electronic design automation (EDA) software and intellectual property (IP) solutions for the semiconductor, automotive, aerospace, and industrial markets. The company's products and services enable designers and engineers to create complex electronic systems and semiconductor chips, driving innovation in areas such as artificial intelligence, 5G, and autonomous vehicles. As a critical enabler of the global electronics industry, Synopsys plays a vital role in advancing technology and improving people's lives.
