About Oracle Corporation

Oracle Corporation is a multinational technology company that provides enterprise software solutions, including database management, cloud computing, and enterprise resource planning. The company's key products and services include the Oracle Database, Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, and Oracle Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Human Capital Management (HCM) solutions. As a leader in the enterprise software market, Oracle helps businesses of all sizes manage their operations, make informed decisions, and drive growth.
