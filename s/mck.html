About McKesson Corporation

McKesson Corporation is a healthcare services and information technology company that provides pharmaceutical and medical-surgical supply chain management solutions, as well as healthcare technology and analytics services. The company offers a range of products and services, including distribution of pharmaceuticals, vaccines, and medical supplies, as well as technology solutions for healthcare providers and payers. As a leading healthcare services company, McKesson plays a critical role in the delivery of patient care and the management of healthcare costs.
