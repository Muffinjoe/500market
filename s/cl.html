About Colgate-Palmolive

Colgate-Palmolive is a multinational consumer goods company that develops, manufactures, and markets a range of personal care and household products, including toothpaste, toothbrushes, soaps, shampoos, and laundry detergents. The company's portfolio of well-known brands includes Colgate, Palmolive, and Irish Spring, among others. As a leading player in the consumer staples sector, Colgate-Palmolive provides essential products to households around the world, driving revenue growth and delivering value to shareholders.
