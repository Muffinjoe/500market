About Prologis

Prologis is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that specializes in the ownership, development, and management of logistics and distribution facilities globally. The company's portfolio includes a diverse range of properties, from traditional warehouses to more specialized facilities such as cold storage and e-commerce distribution centers. As a leading player in the logistics real estate sector, Prologis provides essential infrastructure for global supply chains, supporting the growth of e-commerce and other industries that rely on efficient and reliable distribution networks.
