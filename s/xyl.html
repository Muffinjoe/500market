About Xylem Inc.

Xylem Inc. is a leading global water technology company that designs, manufactures, and services equipment and systems for water and wastewater applications. The company's portfolio includes a range of products and services, including pumps, valves, and treatment systems, as well as software and analytics solutions for water management. By providing innovative solutions for water conservation and sustainability, Xylem plays a critical role in addressing global water challenges and supporting the health and well-being of communities worldwide.
