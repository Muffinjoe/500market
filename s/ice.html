About Intercontinental Exchange

Intercontinental Exchange operates a global network of exchanges, clearing houses, and data services, facilitating the trading of financial, commodity, and currency markets. The company's key products and services include the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), NYSE American, and ICE Futures, as well as data and analytics platforms. As a leading financial infrastructure provider, ICE plays a critical role in the global economy, enabling the efficient and transparent trading of securities and commodities.
