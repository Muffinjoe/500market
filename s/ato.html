About Atmos Energy

Atmos Energy is a leading natural gas distributor in the United States, providing safe and reliable natural gas delivery services to over 3 million residential, commercial, and industrial customers across eight states. The company's key products and services include natural gas distribution, transmission, and storage, as well as infrastructure development and maintenance. As a critical component of the nation's energy infrastructure, Atmos Energy plays a vital role in supporting economic growth, energy security, and environmental sustainability.
