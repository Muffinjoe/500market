About Regions Financial Corporation

Regions Financial Corporation is a bank holding company that provides a range of consumer and commercial banking services through its subsidiary, Regions Bank. The company offers a variety of financial products and services, including deposit accounts, loans, credit cards, and investment services. As one of the largest banks in the United States, Regions Financial plays a significant role in the country's financial infrastructure, serving millions of customers across the southeastern and midwestern regions.
