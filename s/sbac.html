About SBA Communications

SBA Communications is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that specializes in the ownership and operation of wireless communication infrastructure in the United States, Canada, and other countries. The company owns and operates over 42,000 cell towers and other wireless communication sites, providing critical infrastructure for wireless carriers and other tenants. By owning and operating these sites, SBA Communications enables wireless carriers to expand their network coverage and capacity, driving growth in the wireless industry.
