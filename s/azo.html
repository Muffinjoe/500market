About AutoZone

AutoZone is a leading retailer of automotive replacement parts and accessories in the United States. The company operates a chain of stores offering a wide range of products, including new and remanufactured automotive parts, batteries, accessories, and maintenance items. As a major player in the automotive aftermarket industry, AutoZone provides essential products and services to car owners and repair shops, driving growth and profitability in the sector.
