About Verisign

Verisign is a global leader in domain name registration services, providing a range of products and services that enable the operation of the internet's core infrastructure. The company's key offerings include domain name registration, DNS (Domain Name System) services, and cybersecurity solutions, which help protect online identities and transactions. As a critical component of the internet's backbone, Verisign plays a vital role in ensuring the stability and security of online communications.
