About Expeditors International

Expeditors International is a global logistics and freight forwarding company that provides customized supply chain solutions to a diverse base of customers. The company's key services include air and ocean freight forwarding, customs brokerage, warehousing and distribution, and supply chain consulting. Expeditors International plays a critical role in facilitating the movement of goods across the globe, connecting buyers and sellers in a rapidly changing and increasingly complex global marketplace.
