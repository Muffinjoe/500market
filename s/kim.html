About Kimco Realty

Kimco Realty is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates a portfolio of shopping centers and mixed-use properties across the United States. The company's key products and services include property management, leasing, and development of retail and mixed-use properties, with a focus on creating vibrant community spaces. As one of the largest publicly traded REITs in the US, Kimco Realty plays a significant role in shaping the retail landscape and supporting local economies.
