About Corpay

Corpay is a financial technology company that provides a suite of payment and cash management solutions to businesses, enabling them to streamline their payment processes and improve their working capital. The company's key products and services include payment automation, accounts payable and accounts receivable automation, and cash flow management. By simplifying payment operations, Corpay helps businesses reduce costs, increase efficiency, and gain better control over their financial resources.
