About Charles Schwab Corporation

Charles Schwab is a leading financial services company that provides brokerage, banking, and investment services to individuals and institutional clients. The company offers a range of products and services, including brokerage accounts, retirement accounts, investment advice, and banking services, through its online platform and network of retail branches. As one of the largest online brokerages, Schwab matters for its accessibility, low fees, and comprehensive investment solutions, making it a popular choice for individual investors and financial advisors.
