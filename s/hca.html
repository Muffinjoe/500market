About HCA Healthcare

HCA Healthcare is a leading healthcare provider with a network of hospitals and healthcare facilities across the United States. The company offers a range of services, including acute care, outpatient services, and surgical care, through its 185 hospitals and more than 2,000 sites of care. As one of the largest private healthcare providers in the US, HCA plays a significant role in delivering high-quality patient care and advancing healthcare outcomes.
