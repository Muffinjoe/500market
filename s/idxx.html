About Idexx Laboratories

Idexx Laboratories is a global leader in veterinary diagnostics and software for animal health. The company offers a range of products and services, including in-clinic and at-home testing solutions, as well as practice management software and data analytics tools for veterinarians. By providing critical diagnostic information and insights, Idexx Laboratories helps veterinarians improve the health and well-being of animals, driving better outcomes for pet owners and the veterinary community.
