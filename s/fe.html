About FirstEnergy

FirstEnergy is a diversified energy company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity to approximately 6 million customers in the Midwest and Mid-Atlantic regions. The company operates a portfolio of power plants, including nuclear, coal, and natural gas facilities, as well as a network of transmission and distribution lines. As a major player in the US energy sector, FirstEnergy plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing electricity demands.
