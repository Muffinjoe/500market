About NetApp

NetApp is a global leader in cloud data services, providing software, systems, and services to help organizations manage and store their data efficiently. The company's key products and services include its ONTAP operating system, storage systems, and cloud data services, which enable businesses to simplify their data management and accelerate their digital transformation. By providing a unified platform for data management, NetApp helps organizations to improve their agility, reduce costs, and enhance their overall data-driven decision-making capabilities.
