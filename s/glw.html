About Corning Inc.

Corning Inc. is a leading global technology company that specializes in the design, manufacture, and sale of glass, ceramics, and related materials for a range of applications, including telecommunications, consumer electronics, and life sciences. The company's key products include Gorilla Glass for smartphones and tablets, fiber optic cables, and display glass for TVs and monitors. As a key supplier to major technology companies, Corning plays a critical role in enabling the development and deployment of advanced technologies that transform the way people live and work.
