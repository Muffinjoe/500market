About Martin Marietta Materials

Martin Marietta Materials is a leading supplier of aggregates products, including crushed stone, sand, and gravel, as well as a producer of cement and other construction materials. The company serves a diverse range of end markets, including the construction, infrastructure, and energy industries. As a major player in the US aggregates market, Martin Marietta Materials plays a critical role in supporting the nation's infrastructure development and growth.
