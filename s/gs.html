About Goldman Sachs

Goldman Sachs is a multinational investment bank and financial services company that provides a range of products and services to corporations, financial institutions, governments, and high-net-worth individuals. The company's key offerings include investment banking, securities trading, asset management, and consumer and commercial banking. As one of the world's largest and most influential financial institutions, Goldman Sachs plays a significant role in facilitating global economic transactions and shaping market trends.
