About J.M. Smucker Company (The)

The J.M. Smucker Company is a leading manufacturer and marketer of consumer food and beverage products, offering a diverse portfolio of brands including Folgers coffee, Jif peanut butter, and Smucker's fruit spreads. The company's products are sold in the United States, Canada, and internationally, catering to a wide range of consumer preferences and needs. As a prominent player in the consumer staples sector, The J.M. Smucker Company provides essential food and beverage products to households and businesses worldwide.
