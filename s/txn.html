About Texas Instruments

Texas Instruments designs, manufactures, and sells semiconductors and other electronic components, including analog and embedded processing products, as well as wireless connectivity and automation solutions. The company's products are used in a wide range of applications, including industrial automation, automotive systems, medical devices, and consumer electronics. As a leading provider of semiconductor technology, Texas Instruments plays a critical role in enabling innovation and driving growth in the global electronics industry.
