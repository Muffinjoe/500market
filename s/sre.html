About Sempra

Sempra is a multinational energy company that engages in the production, transportation, and sale of electricity, natural gas, and liquefied natural gas. The company's key products and services include electricity generation, natural gas storage, and liquefied natural gas export, with operations in the United States, Mexico, and the United Kingdom. As a leading energy infrastructure company, Sempra plays a critical role in meeting global energy demand and supporting economic growth.
