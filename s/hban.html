About Huntington Bancshares

Huntington Bancshares is a bank holding company that provides a range of financial services, including consumer and commercial banking, through its subsidiary, The Huntington National Bank. The company offers deposit and loan products, payment solutions, and investment services to individuals, businesses, and corporations. As one of the largest banks in the Midwest, Huntington Bancshares plays a significant role in supporting local economic growth and development.
