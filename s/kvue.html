About Kenvue

Kenvue is a multinational consumer healthcare company that develops, manufactures, and markets a range of consumer health products, including pain relief, respiratory, and gastrointestinal medications, as well as feminine care and baby care products. The company's portfolio includes well-known brands such as Tylenol, Neutrogena, and Aquafresh. As a leading player in the consumer staples sector, Kenvue provides essential products that improve the health and well-being of people around the world.
