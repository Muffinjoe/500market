About Vici Properties

Vici Properties is a real estate investment trust that specializes in acquiring and leasing properties to media, entertainment, and gaming companies. Its key assets include iconic venues such as sports stadiums, arenas, and theaters, providing a unique platform for content creation and distribution. By owning and leasing these properties, Vici Properties enables its tenants to focus on their core businesses while generating stable returns for its shareholders.
