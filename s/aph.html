About Amphenol

Amphenol is a leading global designer, manufacturer, and supplier of electrical, electronic and fiber-optic connectors, interconnect systems, antennas, sensors and sensor-based products, and coaxial and high-speed specialty cable. The company's products are used in a wide range of applications, including telecommunications, data communication, industrial, automotive, aerospace and defense, and consumer electronics. As a key supplier to the technology industry, Amphenol plays a critical role in enabling the growth and development of emerging technologies.
