About Freeport-McMoRan

Freeport-McMoRan is a leading international mining company that engages in the exploration, mining, and production of copper, gold, molybdenum, silver, and other minerals. The company operates a diversified portfolio of assets globally, including the Grasberg mine in Indonesia, one of the world's largest copper and gold mines. As a major supplier of copper and other essential minerals, Freeport-McMoRan plays a critical role in supporting global economic growth and technological advancements.
