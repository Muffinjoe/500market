About Genuine Parts Company

Genuine Parts Company is a leading global distributor of automotive replacement parts, industrial replacement parts, and business-to-business products. The company operates through its NAPA Auto Parts, Alltruck Parts, and Motion Industries brands, offering a wide range of products and services to customers in the automotive, industrial, and commercial sectors. As a major player in the automotive and industrial distribution space, Genuine Parts Company plays a vital role in supporting the maintenance and repair needs of businesses and individuals worldwide.
