About Becton Dickinson

Becton Dickinson is a global medical technology company that develops, manufactures, and sells medical devices, diagnostic instruments, and pharmaceutical packaging solutions. Its key products include syringes, needles, and infusion therapy systems, as well as molecular diagnostics and point-of-care testing systems. As a leading provider of medical technology, Becton Dickinson plays a critical role in advancing healthcare outcomes and improving patient care worldwide.
