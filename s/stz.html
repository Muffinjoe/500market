About Constellation Brands

Constellation Brands is a leading beverage alcohol company that produces, imports, and distributes beer, wine, and spirits in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and other international markets. The company's key brands include Corona Extra, Modelo Especial, and Svedka Vodka, among others. As a major player in the beverage alcohol industry, Constellation Brands plays a significant role in shaping consumer preferences and trends in the global market.
