About UnitedHealth Group

UnitedHealth Group is a diversified healthcare company that provides health insurance coverage and health and wellness services to individuals, employers, and governments worldwide. The company offers a range of products and services, including UnitedHealthcare insurance plans, Optum health services, and IngenioRx pharmacy benefit management. As one of the largest health insurers in the US, UnitedHealth Group plays a significant role in shaping the country's healthcare landscape and improving access to quality care.
