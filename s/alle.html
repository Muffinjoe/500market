About Allegion

Allegion is a global security products and solutions provider that offers a range of door and access control systems, electronic locks, and other security-related products and services. The company's products are used in various settings, including commercial, institutional, and residential properties, to enhance safety, security, and convenience. Allegion's solutions help protect people, places, and assets, supporting the growth and development of its customers and the communities they serve.
