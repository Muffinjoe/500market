About Blackstone Inc.

Blackstone Inc is a global investment management firm that provides alternative investment products and financial advisory services to institutional and individual clients. The company offers a range of products, including private equity, real estate, hedge funds, and credit funds, as well as wealth management and financial advisory services. As one of the largest alternative asset managers in the world, Blackstone plays a significant role in shaping the global financial landscape.
