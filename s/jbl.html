About Jabil

Jabil is a global manufacturing solutions company that designs and supplies a wide range of products and services to leading technology companies in the electronics, automotive, industrial, and healthcare industries. The company's portfolio includes contract manufacturing, design and development, supply chain management, and product design services, enabling its customers to bring innovative products to market quickly and efficiently. As a key partner to many leading technology companies, Jabil plays a critical role in the global supply chain, helping to drive innovation and growth in the industries it serves.
