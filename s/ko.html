About Coca-Cola Company (The)

Coca-Cola Company is a multinational beverage corporation that manufactures, markets, and distributes a portfolio of non-alcoholic beverage brands, including Coca-Cola, Fanta, Sprite, and Minute Maid, among others. The company operates in over 200 countries and is one of the largest beverage companies in the world, with a diverse range of products that cater to various tastes and preferences. As a leading consumer staples company, Coca-Cola plays a significant role in the global beverage industry, providing a consistent source of revenue and employment for millions of people worldwide.
