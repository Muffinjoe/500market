About Nordson Corporation

Nordson Corporation is a leading global provider of precision engineered solutions used in the dispensing, processing, and testing of fluids, materials, and other products. The company's products and systems are used in a variety of industries, including aerospace, automotive, medical device, and consumer electronics, to name a few. Nordson's innovative solutions enable customers to improve product quality, reduce costs, and increase efficiency, driving growth and competitiveness in their respective markets.
