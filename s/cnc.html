About Centene Corporation

Centene Corporation is a healthcare company that provides a range of services, including health insurance, pharmacy benefits management, and specialty pharmacy services, primarily to low-income and underinsured individuals. The company operates through its subsidiaries, Ambetter and Fidelis Care, offering Medicaid, Medicare, and the Health Insurance Marketplace plans. As a leading provider of managed care services, Centene plays a critical role in expanding access to healthcare for vulnerable populations.
