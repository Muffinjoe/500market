About WEC Energy Group

WEC Energy Group is a diversified energy company that generates and distributes electricity and natural gas to residential, commercial, and industrial customers in the Midwest and Great Plains regions. The company's key products and services include electricity and natural gas distribution, generation, and transmission, as well as energy storage and renewable energy solutions. As a leading utility company in the United States, WEC Energy Group plays a critical role in powering homes, businesses, and communities across its service area.
