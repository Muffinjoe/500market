About Seagate Technology

Seagate Technology is a global leader in data storage solutions, designing, manufacturing, and marketing hard disk drives (HDDs), solid-state drives (SSDs), and solid-state hybrid drives (SSHDs) for computing, consumer electronics, and enterprise applications. The company's products enable the storage and management of vast amounts of data across various industries, including cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. As a key player in the data storage market, Seagate Technology plays a critical role in facilitating the growth of digital technologies and the increasing demand for data storage solutions.
