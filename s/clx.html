About Clorox

Clorox is a multinational consumer goods company that manufactures and markets a range of household, consumer, and professional products, including cleaning supplies, household chemicals, and food products. Key brands include Clorox bleach, Glad trash bags, and Burt's Bees natural personal care products. As a leading player in the consumer staples sector, Clorox provides essential products for households and businesses worldwide.
