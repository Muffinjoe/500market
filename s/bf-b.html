About Brown–Forman

Brown-Forman Corporation is a leading American spirits and wine company that manufactures and markets a wide range of beverage products globally. Its portfolio includes iconic brands such as Jack Daniel's Tennessee Whiskey, Woodford Reserve, Old Forester, and Finlandia Vodka, among others. As a major player in the spirits industry, Brown-Forman's products are widely available in over 170 countries, making it a significant contributor to the global beverage market.
