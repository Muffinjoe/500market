About Mosaic Company (The)

The Mosaic Company is a leading producer and marketer of concentrated phosphate and potash crop nutrients, serving the global agricultural industry. The company's key products include phosphate-based fertilizers, potash fertilizers, and other nutrient solutions that help farmers increase crop yields and improve food security. As a critical supplier of essential nutrients, Mosaic plays a vital role in meeting the world's growing demand for food and other agricultural products.
