About Exelon

Exelon is a utility company that generates, transmits, and distributes energy to residential, commercial, and industrial customers. Its key products and services include electricity and natural gas, as well as energy efficiency and renewable energy solutions. As one of the largest energy companies in the US, Exelon plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing energy demands while promoting a cleaner and more sustainable energy future.
