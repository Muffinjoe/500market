About Mondelez International

Mondelez International is a multinational confectionery, food, and beverage company that manufactures and distributes a diverse portfolio of iconic brands, including Oreo, Chips Ahoy, Trident, and Cadbury. The company operates in over 150 countries, offering a wide range of products in the snack, baking, and powdered beverage categories. As a leading player in the global consumer goods industry, Mondelez International plays a significant role in shaping consumer preferences and driving innovation in the food and beverage sector.
