About Hewlett Packard Enterprise

Hewlett Packard Enterprise is a multinational information technology company that designs, develops, and sells software, cloud computing, and data storage solutions to businesses and governments worldwide. The company's key products and services include servers, storage systems, networking equipment, and cybersecurity solutions, as well as software for artificial intelligence, machine learning, and the Internet of Things. As a leading provider of enterprise technology, HPE enables businesses to modernize their infrastructure, improve operational efficiency, and drive innovation in the digital age.
