About Interactive Brokers

Interactive Brokers Group is a leading online brokerage firm that provides a range of financial products and services, including brokerage, clearing, and execution services for stocks, options, futures, forex, and cryptocurrencies. The company offers a suite of trading platforms, including its flagship Trader Workstation, as well as mobile and web-based platforms, to support its clients' trading needs. As a low-cost provider with a global reach, Interactive Brokers matters to investors seeking efficient and cost-effective access to global markets.
