About Thermo Fisher Scientific

Thermo Fisher Scientific is a leading life sciences company that develops, manufactures, and markets a wide range of products and services for research, analysis, and diagnostics. Its key offerings include laboratory equipment, reagents, and software for genomics, proteomics, and other life sciences applications, as well as pharmaceutical and biotechnology manufacturing equipment. As a global leader in its industry, Thermo Fisher plays a critical role in advancing scientific research and improving healthcare outcomes.
