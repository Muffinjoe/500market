About Dollar General

Dollar General operates a chain of variety retail stores offering a range of products, including groceries, household essentials, and general merchandise, primarily targeting price-conscious consumers in the United States. The company's stores are typically smaller than traditional retailers, allowing for lower prices and a convenient shopping experience. As a leading player in the discount retail space, Dollar General provides essential goods to underserved communities and competes with other discount retailers.
