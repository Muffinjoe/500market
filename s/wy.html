About Weyerhaeuser

Weyerhaeuser is a leading timber, land, and forest products company that operates in the United States, Canada, and other countries. The company's key products include lumber, oriented strand board (OSB), engineered wood products, and pulp. As one of the largest forestland owners in the United States, Weyerhaeuser plays a significant role in the global supply chain for wood products and sustainable forest management.
