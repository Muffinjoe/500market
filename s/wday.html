About Workday, Inc.

Workday, Inc. is a cloud-based financial management and human capital management software provider that offers a range of enterprise software solutions to help organizations manage their financial, human capital, and planning processes. The company's key products include Workday Financial Management, Workday Human Capital Management, and Workday Planning, which enable businesses to streamline their operations, improve decision-making, and enhance employee engagement. Workday's solutions are used by over 5,000 organizations worldwide, making it a leading player in the cloud-based enterprise software market.
