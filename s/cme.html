About CME Group

CME Group is a global derivatives marketplace and clearinghouse that operates a range of financial exchanges, including the Chicago Mercantile Exchange, the Chicago Board of Trade, and the New York Mercantile Exchange. The company offers futures and options contracts on a variety of assets, including commodities, currencies, interest rates, and equity indexes, facilitating price discovery and risk management for market participants. As a leading provider of derivatives trading and clearing services, CME Group plays a critical role in global financial markets, enabling the efficient transfer of risk and facilitating the flow of capital.
