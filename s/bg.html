About Bunge Global

Bunge Global is a leading agribusiness and food company that sources, processes, and distributes food and feed ingredients globally. The company's key products and services include oilseed processing, grains and oilseeds trading, and edible oil refining, as well as sugar, bioenergy, and fertilizers. As a major player in the global food supply chain, Bunge Global plays a critical role in ensuring food security and stability for consumers worldwide.
