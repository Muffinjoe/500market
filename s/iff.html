About International Flavors & Fragrances

International Flavors & Fragrances is a global leader in the creation of flavors and fragrances for the food, beverage, personal care, and household products industries. The company develops and manufactures a diverse portfolio of products, including fragrances, flavor ingredients, and functional ingredients, which are used by leading consumer goods companies worldwide. As a key supplier of ingredients to the global food, beverage, and personal care industries, IFF plays a critical role in shaping consumer preferences and driving innovation in these sectors.
