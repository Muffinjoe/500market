About CMS Energy

CMS Energy is a diversified energy company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity and natural gas to residential, commercial, and industrial customers in Michigan. The company's key products and services include electricity and natural gas supply, transmission and distribution, and energy storage solutions. As a leading utility provider in the Midwest, CMS Energy plays a critical role in meeting the region's energy needs and contributing to a reliable and sustainable energy infrastructure.
