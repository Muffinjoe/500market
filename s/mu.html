About Micron Technology

Micron Technology is a leading global manufacturer of semiconductor devices, including DRAM, NAND flash memory, and other memory and storage solutions. The company's products are used in a wide range of applications, from consumer electronics and data centers to automotive and industrial systems. As a key supplier of memory and storage solutions, Micron Technology plays a critical role in enabling the development of emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things, and 5G networks.
