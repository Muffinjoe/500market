About Uber

Uber Technologies, Inc. is a technology company that provides ride-hailing, food delivery, and other mobility services through its platform. The company's key products and services include Uber Ride, Uber Eats, Uber Freight, and Uber Movement, among others. As a leading player in the gig economy, Uber has transformed the way people and goods move, making it easier and more convenient to get around cities worldwide.
