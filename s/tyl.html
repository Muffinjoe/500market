About Tyler Technologies

Tyler Technologies is a provider of integrated software and technology services focused on the public sector, offering a range of products and services that support the administrative, financial, and operational needs of local governments, schools, and other public entities. The company's key offerings include municipal and county software solutions, court case management, and public safety software, among others. By streamlining processes and improving efficiency, Tyler Technologies helps public sector organizations enhance citizen engagement, reduce costs, and improve overall service delivery.
