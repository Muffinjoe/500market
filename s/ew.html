About Edwards Lifesciences

Edwards Lifesciences is a global leader in the development, manufacture, and marketing of innovative medical solutions for minimally invasive procedures in cardiology, critical care, and surgical specialties. The company's key products include heart valves, transcatheter heart valves, and critical care monitoring systems, which help improve patient outcomes and quality of life. Edwards Lifesciences plays a critical role in advancing the treatment of cardiovascular disease and critical care conditions.
