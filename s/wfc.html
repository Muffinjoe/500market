About Wells Fargo

Wells Fargo is a multinational financial services company providing banking, investment, mortgage, and consumer finance products to individuals, businesses, and institutions. The company offers a range of services, including checking and savings accounts, credit cards, home loans, and investment management. As one of the largest banks in the US, Wells Fargo plays a significant role in the country's financial system, serving over 70 million customers worldwide.
