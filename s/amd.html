About Advanced Micro Devices

Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) is a leading semiconductor company that designs, manufactures, and markets microprocessors, graphics processing units (GPUs), and other computer hardware solutions for the gaming, professional visualization, datacenter, and consumer electronics markets. The company's products are used in a wide range of applications, including personal computers, servers, gaming consoles, and mobile devices. As a key competitor to Intel, AMD plays a significant role in shaping the global technology landscape and driving innovation in the semiconductor industry.
