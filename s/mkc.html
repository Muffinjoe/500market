About McCormick & Company

McCormick & Company is a global leader in the manufacture, marketing, and distribution of spices, seasonings, and condiments, offering a wide range of products including spices, baking supplies, and specialty foods. The company's portfolio includes well-known brands such as McCormick, French's, and Old Bay, among others. As a leading player in the consumer staples industry, McCormick & Company provides essential ingredients to households and foodservice operators worldwide, driving flavor and convenience in everyday meals.
