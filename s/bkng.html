About Booking Holdings

Booking Holdings is a leading digital travel company that operates several well-known online travel agencies, including Booking.com, Priceline.com, Agoda.com, and Kayak.com. These platforms provide users with access to a vast inventory of travel products, including accommodations, flights, car rentals, and package deals. By connecting travelers with a wide range of options, Booking Holdings facilitates the booking process and drives growth in the global travel industry.
