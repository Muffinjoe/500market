About Humana

Humana is a health care company that offers a range of products and services, including Medicare Advantage and commercial health insurance, as well as health and wellness services. The company's key products and services include pharmacy benefit management, care coordination, and health information technology solutions. As one of the largest health insurance companies in the United States, Humana plays a significant role in providing access to healthcare for millions of Americans.
