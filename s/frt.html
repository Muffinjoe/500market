About Federal Realty Investment Trust

Federal Realty Investment Trust is a real estate investment trust that owns and operates a diversified portfolio of retail and mixed-use properties across the United States. The company's portfolio includes high-quality shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment venues, as well as office and residential properties. By providing a unique blend of retail, dining, and entertainment options, Federal Realty aims to create vibrant community hubs that drive economic growth and enhance the quality of life for its customers and tenants.
