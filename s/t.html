About AT&T

AT&T Inc. is a multinational conglomerate providing a range of communication, media, and technology services to consumers and businesses worldwide. The company offers a diverse portfolio of products and services, including wireless voice and data services, video and broadband services, and advertising and sponsorship services through its WarnerMedia subsidiary. As one of the largest telecommunications companies globally, AT&T plays a significant role in shaping the digital landscape and connecting people, businesses, and communities.
