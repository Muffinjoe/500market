About Illinois Tool Works

Illinois Tool Works is a diversified multinational manufacturer of industrial products and equipment, offering a wide range of solutions to various industries including food equipment, construction, and automotive. Key products include fasteners, food processing equipment, and hydraulic systems, among others. As a leading industrial company, ITW plays a significant role in supporting global manufacturing and infrastructure development.
