About PulteGroup

PulteGroup is a leading American homebuilder and land developer, operating in the United States and Canada. The company designs, builds, and sells new homes in over 40 markets across the country, offering a range of housing options from entry-level to luxury, through its brands Pulte Homes, Del Webb, and DiVosta. As a major player in the US housing market, PulteGroup's performance provides insight into the country's housing demand and economic health.
