About Gartner

Gartner is a global research and advisory company that provides information technology (IT) research and analysis to business leaders, IT professionals, and technology companies. The company offers a range of products and services, including research reports, advisory services, and conferences, to help clients navigate complex IT landscapes and make informed decisions. By providing objective and actionable insights, Gartner helps organizations optimize their IT strategies and stay ahead of the competition in a rapidly evolving technology landscape.
