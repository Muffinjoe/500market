About Solventum

Solventum is a leading developer of innovative medical diagnostic technologies, specializing in the design and manufacturing of laboratory equipment and reagents for the detection of infectious diseases. The company's flagship products include the Solventum Analyzer, a high-performance instrument used to detect and quantify various biomarkers, and its line of proprietary reagents, which enhance the accuracy and efficiency of diagnostic testing. By providing critical tools for disease diagnosis and surveillance, Solventum plays a vital role in public health efforts worldwide.
