About AppLovin

AppLovin is a leading mobile gaming and monetization company that provides a suite of solutions for developers and publishers to grow their mobile apps and games. Its key products and services include the AppLovin MAX platform, which offers a unified marketplace for mobile ad inventory, and the IronSource platform, which provides a range of monetization tools for mobile games. By helping developers and publishers succeed, AppLovin plays a critical role in the mobile gaming ecosystem and drives innovation in the industry.
