About Moody's Corporation

Moody's Corporation is a leading provider of credit ratings, research, and risk analysis for financial markets, offering insights and data to help investors, financial institutions, and corporations make informed decisions. The company's key products and services include credit ratings, credit research, and risk management solutions, as well as Moody's Analytics, a leading provider of financial intelligence. Moody's plays a critical role in maintaining the integrity of global financial markets by providing independent and objective credit assessments.
