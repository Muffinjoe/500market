About DuPont

DuPont is a multinational corporation that develops and manufactures a range of materials and products, including chemicals, materials science, and agriculture solutions. The company's key products include Kevlar, Tyvek, and Sorona, as well as crop protection and seeds. As a leading materials science company, DuPont plays a crucial role in driving innovation and sustainability across various industries.
