About IDEX Corporation

IDEX Corporation is a leading global provider of pumps, valves, and other fluid-handling equipment and engineered solutions for various industries, including food and beverage, chemical, and pharmaceutical. The company's product portfolio includes brands such as Almatec, Gorman-Rupp, and IDEX Flowtronics, among others. As a key supplier to various manufacturing sectors, IDEX plays a vital role in ensuring the efficient production and processing of goods.
