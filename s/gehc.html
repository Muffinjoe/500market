About GE HealthCare

GE HealthCare is a leading global health care company that provides medical imaging, diagnostics, patient care, and medical research technologies. The company offers a range of products and services, including MRI and CT scanners, ultrasound systems, and patient monitoring solutions, as well as informatics and analytics tools. By advancing health care technology, GE HealthCare enables healthcare providers to make more informed decisions and improve patient outcomes.
