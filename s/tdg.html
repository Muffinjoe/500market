About TransDigm Group

TransDigm Group is a leading global designer, producer, and supplier of highly engineered aerospace components, systems, and subsystems. The company's products and services cater to the commercial and military aerospace markets, including aircraft systems, power and control systems, and other specialized components. TransDigm's offerings enable the safe and efficient operation of aircraft, supporting the global air transportation network and military operations.
