About Texas Pacific Land Corporation

Texas Pacific Land Corporation is a leading landowner and real estate investment trust (REIT) focused on the acquisition, development, and sale of land in the Permian Basin of West Texas. The company primarily engages in the sale of land to oil and gas operators, providing a critical component of the upstream energy supply chain. As a key player in the Permian Basin, TPL plays a vital role in supporting the growth and development of the US oil and gas industry.
