About Stanley Black & Decker

Stanley Black & Decker is a leading global provider of tools and storage, security, and engineered fastening systems. The company offers a diverse portfolio of iconic brands, including Stanley, Black+Decker, DeWalt, and Facom, among others, which cater to professionals and DIYers in the construction, automotive, and industrial sectors. As a key player in the global industrial market, Stanley Black & Decker's innovative products and solutions drive productivity, efficiency, and safety in various industries.
