About Avery Dennison

Avery Dennison Corporation is a global materials science and manufacturing company that designs, manufactures, and markets a range of products and solutions for various industries, including labeling and packaging, materials science, and retail branding. The company's key products and services include pressure-sensitive materials, RFID inlays, and digital printing solutions, among others. Avery Dennison's innovative materials and technologies enable businesses to enhance their brand experiences, improve operational efficiency, and drive sustainability.
