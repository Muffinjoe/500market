About Cigna

Cigna is a global health services organization that provides medical, dental, disability, and life insurance products and services to individuals, families, and employers. The company's key offerings include health insurance, pharmacy benefits management, and Medicare and Medicaid plans, as well as a range of specialty health services. As a major player in the health insurance industry, Cigna plays a significant role in shaping the delivery of healthcare services and improving health outcomes for its customers.
