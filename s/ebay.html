About eBay Inc.

eBay Inc. is a global e-commerce company that operates a platform for the sale of goods and services by third-party sellers. The company's key products and services include its online marketplace, PayPal payment processing, and various mobile apps. As a leading online marketplace, eBay connects buyers and sellers worldwide, facilitating the exchange of goods and services and driving consumer spending.
