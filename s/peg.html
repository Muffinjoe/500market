About Public Service Enterprise Group

Public Service Enterprise Group (PEG) is a diversified energy company that operates in the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic regions of the United States, providing electricity and gas to approximately 10 million customers. The company generates, transmits, and distributes electricity, as well as supplies gas to residential, commercial, and industrial customers. As a leading utility company, PEG plays a critical role in meeting the energy needs of its customers and supporting economic growth in the regions it serves.
