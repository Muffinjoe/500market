About Motorola Solutions

Motorola Solutions designs, manufactures, and supplies mission-critical communication solutions and services for public safety and commercial customers. The company's key products and services include two-way radios, body-worn cameras, and software platforms for public safety, as well as broadband and 5G network infrastructure for commercial customers. Motorola Solutions plays a critical role in enhancing public safety and enabling secure, reliable communication for emergency responders and critical infrastructure operations.
