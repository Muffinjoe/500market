About Procter & Gamble

Procter & Gamble is a multinational consumer goods corporation that develops and markets a diverse portfolio of consumer staples products, including household care, beauty, and personal care items such as Tide laundry detergent, Gillette razors, and Pantene hair care products. The company operates globally, serving over 5 billion consumers in nearly 180 countries. As a leader in the consumer staples sector, Procter & Gamble's products are staples in many households worldwide, providing essential goods and services to consumers.
