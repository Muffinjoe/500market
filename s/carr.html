About Carrier Global

Carrier Global Corporation is a leading global provider of healthy, safe, and sustainable building and refrigeration solutions, offering a diverse portfolio of products and services that include air conditioning, heating, ventilation, refrigeration, and fire and security systems. The company's solutions are used in various industries, including commercial, industrial, and residential markets, and are designed to improve indoor air quality, energy efficiency, and occupant comfort. As a leader in the built environment, Carrier Global plays a critical role in shaping the future of sustainable and resilient buildings.
