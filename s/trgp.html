About Targa Resources

Targa Resources is a midstream energy company that operates a network of pipelines, storage facilities, and terminals primarily in the United States. The company provides natural gas and natural gas liquids (NGLs) gathering, processing, and transportation services, as well as NGLs fractionation and marketing services. As a leading midstream provider, Targa Resources plays a critical role in connecting the production of oil and natural gas to the markets that consume it.
