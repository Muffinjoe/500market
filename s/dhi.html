About D. R. Horton

D. R. Horton is a leading homebuilder in the United States, operating in 27 states and 84 markets. The company designs, builds, and sells single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums through its various brands, catering to a wide range of consumers. As one of the largest homebuilders in the country, D. R. Horton plays a significant role in shaping the US housing market and providing affordable housing options for millions of Americans.
