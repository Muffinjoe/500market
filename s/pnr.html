About Pentair

Pentair is a global industrial company that designs, manufactures, and markets industrial and commercial water treatment and flow control solutions, as well as valves and controls. The company's key products and services include residential and commercial pool equipment, water filtration systems, and valves and controls for various industries. By providing innovative solutions for water management and flow control, Pentair plays a critical role in supporting global water conservation and infrastructure development.
