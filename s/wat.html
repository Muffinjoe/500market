About Waters Corporation

Waters Corporation is a global leader in analytical instrumentation and laboratory software, providing innovative solutions for life sciences, environmental, food safety, and industrial markets. The company's products include chromatography systems, mass spectrometry systems, thermal analysis systems, and laboratory software, enabling customers to analyze and understand complex biological and chemical samples. By advancing analytical science, Waters Corporation helps researchers, scientists, and healthcare professionals make new discoveries and improve patient outcomes.
