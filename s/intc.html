About Intel

Intel Corporation is a technology company that designs, manufactures, and sells semiconductor chips and related products, including microprocessors, chipsets, and other interconnect products. The company's products are used in a wide range of applications, including personal computers, data centers, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT). As a leader in the semiconductor industry, Intel plays a critical role in enabling the development of innovative technologies and driving the growth of the global digital economy.
