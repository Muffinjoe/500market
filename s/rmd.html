About ResMed

ResMed is a medical device company that develops, manufactures, and distributes medical equipment for the diagnosis, treatment, and management of sleep-related breathing disorders, as well as other respiratory diseases. The company's key products include sleep apnea and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) devices, such as continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) machines and oxygen concentrators. ResMed's innovative solutions improve patient outcomes and quality of life, driving growth in the healthcare industry and enhancing the company's position as a leader in the medical device sector.
