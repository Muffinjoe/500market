About Eversource Energy

Eversource Energy is a leading energy delivery company that provides electricity, natural gas, and water services to residential, commercial, and industrial customers in the United States. The company operates through three main segments: electric, gas, and water, offering essential energy and water services to over 4 million customers across Connecticut, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Rhode Island. As a critical component of the nation's energy infrastructure, Eversource Energy plays a vital role in powering homes, businesses, and communities across the Northeast region.
