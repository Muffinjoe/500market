About Lamb Weston

Lamb Weston Holdings, Inc., a leading global producer and marketer of frozen potato products, offers a diverse portfolio of frozen potato, sweet potato, and other vegetable products. The company's key products include frozen french fries, hash browns, and other potato specialties, serving major foodservice and retail customers worldwide. As a leading player in the frozen potato market, Lamb Weston plays a critical role in meeting the growing demand for convenient, high-quality frozen food products.
