About DoorDash

DoorDash is a technology company that provides food delivery and logistics services through its platform, connecting consumers with local restaurants and food establishments. The company's key products and services include its on-demand delivery platform, DashPass subscription service, and DasherDirect payment platform. By streamlining food delivery and expanding access to local cuisine, DoorDash has become a leading player in the rapidly growing food delivery market.
