About Cincinnati Financial

Cincinnati Financial Corporation is a property casualty insurance holding company that provides commercial and personal insurance products through its subsidiaries, including The Cincinnati Insurance Company, Insurance Company of the Midwest, and other affiliates. The company offers a range of insurance products, including commercial property, casualty, and workers' compensation insurance, as well as personal auto, homeowners, and umbrella insurance. Cincinnati Financial's products and services help individuals and businesses manage risk and protect their assets.
