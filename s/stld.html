About Steel Dynamics

Steel Dynamics is a leading domestic steel producer and metals recycling company. The company operates a portfolio of steel production facilities, steel finishing facilities, and metals recycling facilities, producing a range of steel products, including flat roll steel, long roll steel, and steel tubing. As a major player in the US steel industry, Steel Dynamics plays a critical role in supporting the country's infrastructure development and manufacturing sectors.
