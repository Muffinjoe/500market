About Cardinal Health

Cardinal Health is a multinational healthcare services and products company that provides pharmaceuticals, medical supplies, and health information technology solutions to hospitals, pharmacies, and healthcare providers. The company offers a range of products and services, including generic and branded medications, medical and surgical supplies, and clinical and business process outsourcing solutions. As a leading healthcare distributor, Cardinal Health plays a critical role in ensuring the availability and accessibility of essential healthcare products and services.
