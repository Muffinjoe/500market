About Cooper Companies (The)

The Cooper Companies, Inc. is a global medical device company that designs, manufactures, and markets a range of contact lenses and eye care products, including intraocular lenses and surgical instruments. The company's key products include soft contact lenses, rigid gas permeable contact lenses, and intraocular lenses used in cataract surgery. As a leading provider of eye care solutions, Cooper Companies plays a critical role in improving vision and enhancing the quality of life for patients worldwide.
