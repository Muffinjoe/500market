About KLA Corporation

KLA Corporation is a leading global supplier of process control and yield management solutions for the semiconductor and data storage industries. The company's portfolio includes inspection and metrology systems, process control solutions, and yield management products that enable customers to improve manufacturing efficiency and product quality. By providing critical data and insights, KLA helps drive innovation and advancement in the fields of computing, communications, and data storage.
