About Zoetis

Zoetis is a leading global animal health company that develops, manufactures, and commercializes a wide range of veterinary vaccines, medicines, and diagnostics for livestock and companion animals. The company's product portfolio includes prescription and over-the-counter medications, as well as vaccines and biologic products for various species, including cattle, pigs, poultry, and companion animals. By providing innovative solutions to improve animal health and well-being, Zoetis plays a critical role in supporting the global food supply and advancing the human-animal bond.
