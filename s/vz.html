About Verizon

Verizon Communications Inc is a leading provider of wireless and wireline communication services in the United States. The company offers a range of products and services, including wireless voice and data services, broadband internet, and television services, as well as cloud and security solutions. As one of the largest telecommunications companies in the US, Verizon plays a critical role in connecting people, businesses, and communities through its extensive network infrastructure.
