About Copart

Copart is a global online vehicle auction company that provides a platform for buyers and sellers to connect and conduct auctions for used and damaged vehicles. The company's services include online auctions, vehicle inspection reports, and transportation services, catering to a diverse range of customers including insurance companies, dealerships, and individual buyers. By providing a convenient and efficient platform for vehicle sales, Copart plays a significant role in the automotive industry, helping to manage inventory and facilitate the resale of vehicles.
