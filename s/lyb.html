About LyondellBasell

LyondellBasell Industries N.V. is a multinational chemical company that produces a wide range of products including polyethylene, polypropylene, and propylene oxide, which are used in various industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive. The company's products are used in the production of plastics, fibers, and other materials that are essential for modern life. As a leading producer of petrochemicals and polymers, LyondellBasell plays a critical role in meeting the growing demand for sustainable and innovative materials.
