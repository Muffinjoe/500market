About Fair Isaac

Fair Isaac Corporation, operating under the ticker FICO, is a leading provider of analytics and decision management technology solutions. The company's key products and services include credit scoring models, fraud detection software, and customer management systems, which help businesses and financial institutions make informed decisions. By providing data-driven insights, FICO enables its clients to optimize their operations, reduce risk, and improve customer experiences, ultimately driving growth and profitability.
