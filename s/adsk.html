About Autodesk

Autodesk is a global leader in the design, development, and manufacturing of software for building information modeling, computer-aided design, computer-aided engineering, and computer-aided manufacturing. The company's flagship products include AutoCAD, Revit, Inventor, and Fusion 360, which enable architects, engineers, and manufacturers to create, visualize, and simulate digital models of real-world objects. By providing innovative software solutions, Autodesk empowers users to design, build, and deliver innovative projects more efficiently and sustainably.
