About Mid-America Apartment Communities

Mid-America Apartment Communities is a multifamily real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates apartment communities across the United States. The company's portfolio consists of approximately 100,000 apartment homes across 14 states, offering residents a range of amenities and services. As a leading owner and operator of apartment communities, Mid-America Apartment Communities provides a vital component of the housing market, offering a stable source of income for investors and a home for thousands of residents.
