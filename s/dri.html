About Darden Restaurants

Darden Restaurants is a leading operator of casual dining restaurants in the United States, owning and franchising a diverse portfolio of brands including Olive Garden, LongHorn Steakhouse, and Cheddar's Scratch Kitchen. The company's restaurants offer a range of menu options, from Italian and American comfort food to steakhouses and casual family dining. As a prominent player in the casual dining industry, Darden Restaurants provides a convenient and affordable dining experience for millions of customers across the country.
