About Trane Technologies

Trane Technologies is a global climate control company that designs, manufactures, and markets heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems, as well as refrigeration and building services. The company's portfolio includes brands such as Trane, Thermo King, and American Standard, offering a range of products and solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. As a leader in the HVAC industry, Trane Technologies plays a critical role in improving indoor air quality, energy efficiency, and sustainability for buildings and communities worldwide.
