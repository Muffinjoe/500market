About Williams Companies

Williams Companies is a leading energy infrastructure company that operates through three primary segments: Gas Gathering and Processing, Gas Pipelines, and NGL and Petchem Services. The company transports and processes natural gas, as well as produces and sells natural gas liquids (NGLs) and petchem products. As a critical component of the US energy infrastructure, Williams Companies plays a vital role in connecting domestic energy production to markets, supporting the country's energy independence and economic growth.
