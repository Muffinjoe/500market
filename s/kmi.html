About Kinder Morgan

Kinder Morgan is a leading energy infrastructure company that operates a vast network of pipelines, terminals, and storage facilities, transporting and storing natural gas, crude oil, and other petroleum products across North America. The company's key products and services include pipeline transportation, terminal and storage operations, and carbon capture and storage solutions. As a critical component of the energy infrastructure, Kinder Morgan plays a vital role in connecting producers with consumers, supporting the growth of the energy industry, and promoting economic development.
