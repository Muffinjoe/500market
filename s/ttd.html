About Trade Desk (The)

The Trade Desk is a global technology company that provides a self-service, cloud-based platform for data consumers to buy and sell data-driven advertising. The company's platform, known as the Trade Desk, offers a range of products and services, including data management, ad serving, and attribution analytics, allowing advertisers to target and measure their campaigns across various digital channels. By providing a more efficient and effective way to buy and sell data-driven advertising, The Trade Desk has become a key player in the digital advertising ecosystem.
