About Phillips 66

Phillips 66 is an energy manufacturing and logistics company that refines, transports, and markets petroleum products, as well as produces chemicals and fuels. The company's key products include gasoline, diesel fuel, jet fuel, and lubricants, which are distributed through a network of refineries, pipelines, and terminals. As a leading energy company, Phillips 66 plays a crucial role in meeting global energy demands and supporting economic growth.
