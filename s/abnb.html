About Airbnb

Airbnb is a global online marketplace for short-term vacation rentals, allowing users to book unique accommodations, experiences, and activities. The company's platform connects hosts who offer their properties, such as apartments, houses, and villas, with travelers seeking alternative lodging options. As a leader in the sharing economy, Airbnb has disrupted the traditional hospitality industry and transformed the way people travel and experience new destinations.
