About Cisco

Cisco Systems, Inc. is a technology company that designs, manufactures, and sells networking hardware, software, and telecommunications equipment. The company's key products and services include routers, switches, network security systems, and collaboration tools, which enable businesses and organizations to connect, communicate, and operate more efficiently. As a leading provider of networking solutions, Cisco plays a critical role in facilitating global communication and digital transformation.
