About Globe Life

Globe Life is a holding company that operates as a life and supplemental health insurance company, primarily offering individual supplemental insurance products and annuities. Its key products include life insurance, accident insurance, and fixed annuities, which provide financial protection and savings solutions to policyholders. As a leading provider of supplemental insurance, Globe Life plays a significant role in helping individuals and families manage financial risks and achieve long-term financial security.
