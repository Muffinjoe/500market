About Honeywell

Honeywell International Inc. is a diversified multinational conglomerate that develops and manufactures a range of products and solutions for various industries, including aerospace, building technologies, and industrial automation. The company's key products and services include aircraft engines, industrial automation systems, security and surveillance equipment, and building management systems. As a leading provider of innovative technologies, Honeywell plays a critical role in shaping the future of industries and improving the efficiency and safety of operations worldwide.
