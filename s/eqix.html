About Equinix

Equinix is a global interconnection and data center company that provides colocation, interconnection, and managed services to businesses and organizations. The company operates a network of over 240 data centers across more than 70 markets worldwide, offering a range of products and services that enable customers to connect, interconnect, and distribute digital content, applications, and services. By providing a platform for digital businesses to scale and grow, Equinix plays a critical role in the global digital economy.
