About Target Corporation

Target Corporation is a multinational retailing company that operates a chain of general merchandise stores offering a wide range of products, including clothing, home goods, electronics, and groceries. The company's stores provide a one-stop shopping experience for customers, offering a variety of products from national and private-label brands. As one of the largest retailers in the United States, Target plays a significant role in shaping consumer spending habits and trends.
