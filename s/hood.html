About Robinhood Markets

Robinhood Markets is a financial technology company that offers commission-free trading and investing services to retail customers through its mobile app and website. The company's key products and services include its self-guided trading platform, cryptocurrency trading, and cash management accounts. By democratizing access to the financial markets, Robinhood has disrupted the traditional brokerage industry and made investing more accessible to a wider range of consumers.
