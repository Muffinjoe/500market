About Alexandria Real Estate Equities

Alexandria Real Estate Equities is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, operates, and develops life science and technology campuses primarily on the West Coast of the United States. The company focuses on providing high-quality, specialized properties to pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and life science companies, as well as technology and biotechnology research institutions. By offering customized facilities and amenities, Alexandria Real Estate Equities enables its tenants to advance their research and development efforts, driving innovation and growth in the life science and technology sectors.
