About Crown Castle

Crown Castle is a real estate investment trust that owns, operates, and leases cell towers and other wireless infrastructure across the United States and Australia. The company's portfolio consists of over 40,000 cell towers and 80,000 small cells, providing critical connectivity infrastructure to wireless carriers and other tenants. Crown Castle's assets support the growing demand for wireless data and enable the expansion of 5G networks, making it a key player in the telecommunications industry.
