About Labcorp

Labcorp is a leading global life sciences company that provides clinical laboratory services, genomic testing, and medical diagnostics to healthcare professionals, pharmaceutical companies, and patients. The company's comprehensive range of testing services includes genetic testing, infectious disease testing, and cancer diagnostics, among others. By providing critical diagnostic information, Labcorp helps healthcare professionals make informed decisions, improve patient outcomes, and advance medical research.
