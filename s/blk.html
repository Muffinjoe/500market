About BlackRock

Blackrock is a global investment management corporation that provides a range of investment and risk management solutions to institutional and retail clients. The company's key products and services include index funds, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), and actively managed investment portfolios. As one of the world's largest asset managers, Blackrock plays a significant role in shaping the global financial landscape and influencing investment trends.
