About Dover Corporation

Dover Corporation is a diversified global industrial company that operates through various segments, including Engineered Systems, Clean Energy & Fueling, Imaging & Identification, Pumps & Instruments, and Fluids. Dover's portfolio includes a range of products and services, including pumps, compressors, fluid management equipment, and imaging and identification solutions. The company's diverse offerings enable customers across various industries to improve efficiency, productivity, and safety, making it a significant player in the global industrial landscape.
