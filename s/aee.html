About Ameren

Ameren is a diversified energy company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity to approximately 2.4 million customers in Missouri and Illinois. The company's key products and services include electricity generation from coal, natural gas, and renewable sources, as well as natural gas distribution and transmission. As a major player in the U.S. utilities sector, Ameren plays a critical role in meeting the energy needs of its customers and supporting economic growth in the regions it serves.
