About RTX Corporation

Raytheon Technologies is a multinational conglomerate that designs, develops, and manufactures advanced aerospace and defense systems, including commercial and military aircraft, missiles, and cybersecurity solutions. The company's key products and services include the F-35 fighter jet, the Patriot missile defense system, and Pratt & Whitney commercial aircraft engines. As a leading player in the aerospace and defense industry, Raytheon Technologies plays a critical role in supporting national security, advancing technological innovation, and driving economic growth.
