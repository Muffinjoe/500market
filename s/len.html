About Lennar

Lennar Corporation is a leading homebuilder and real estate developer in the United States, specializing in the construction and sale of single-family homes, condominiums, and apartment communities. The company operates in 21 states and Puerto Rico, offering a range of home designs and price points to meet diverse customer needs. As a major player in the US housing market, Lennar's performance is closely watched as an indicator of the broader housing sector and the overall health of the US economy.
