About Arista Networks

Arista Networks designs, manufactures, and sells cloud networking solutions that are used to build software-defined cloud networks. The company's products include Ethernet switches, routers, and software that enable customers to build and manage scalable, high-performance networks. Arista's solutions are used by cloud service providers, data centers, and enterprises to improve network efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance application performance.
