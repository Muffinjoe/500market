About Cummins

Cummins Inc. is a global power technology leader that designs, manufactures, and distributes a wide range of power solutions, including diesel and natural gas engines, electric and hybrid powertrains, filtration, and power generation products. The company serves customers in the heavy-duty truck, bus, construction, agricultural, and industrial markets, as well as the energy and oil and gas industries. As a leading provider of clean and efficient power solutions, Cummins plays a critical role in enabling the transition to a more sustainable and environmentally friendly transportation and energy landscape.
