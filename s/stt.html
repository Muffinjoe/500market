About State Street Corporation

State Street Corporation is a financial services and investment management company that provides a range of products and services to institutional investors, financial institutions, and individuals, including custody, investment management, and investment research. The company offers a variety of key products and services, including index funds, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), and alternative investments. As one of the largest custodian banks in the world, State Street plays a critical role in the global financial system by providing secure and efficient management of financial assets.
