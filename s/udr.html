About UDR, Inc.

UDR, Inc. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, acquires, renovates, develops, and manages multifamily apartment communities primarily in the United States. The company's key products and services include the ownership and operation of over 130 properties containing more than 58,000 apartments across 26 states and the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. UDR's business model provides a stable source of income for investors through rental income and property appreciation.
