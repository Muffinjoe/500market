About Abbott Laboratories

Abbott Laboratories is a diversified global healthcare company that develops, manufactures, and markets a range of products, including diagnostics, medical devices, nutritionals, and branded generic pharmaceuticals. The company's key products include its FreeStyle glucose monitoring systems, MitraClip transcatheter mitral valve repair system, and Similac infant formula. As a leading healthcare company, Abbott Laboratories plays a significant role in improving patient outcomes and enhancing quality of life through its innovative products and technologies.
