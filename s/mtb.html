About M&T Bank

M&T Bank is a financial holding company that provides commercial and retail banking services to individuals, businesses, and institutions through its network of branches and ATMs. The company offers a range of products and services, including deposit accounts, credit cards, mortgages, commercial lending, and wealth management solutions. As one of the largest regional banks in the United States, M&T Bank plays a significant role in supporting economic growth and financial stability in the communities it serves.
