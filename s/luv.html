About Southwest Airlines

Southwest Airlines is a major United States-based low-cost carrier that operates a network of domestic and international scheduled air transportation services. The company's key products include scheduled air transportation, cargo services, and aircraft leasing. As one of the largest airlines in the world, Southwest Airlines plays a significant role in connecting people and businesses across the globe, driving economic growth and development.
