About Broadridge Financial Solutions

Broadridge Financial Solutions is a technology-enabled financial services company providing communications, technology, and data and analytics solutions to the financial services industry. Its key products and services include investor communication and proxy solicitation, data and analytics, and securities processing and clearing. By enabling the secure and efficient exchange of information, Broadridge plays a critical role in facilitating the operations of the financial services industry.
