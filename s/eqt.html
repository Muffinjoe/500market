About EQT Corporation

EQT Corporation is a leading independent natural gas production company in the United States, primarily focused on the Appalachian region. The company operates through its subsidiaries, EQT Production and EQT Midstream Partners, to explore, produce, and transport natural gas. EQT plays a significant role in meeting the country's growing energy demands, with a strong presence in the domestic natural gas market.
