About Simon Property Group

Simon Property Group is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, develops, and manages a portfolio of retail and mixed-use properties across the United States and internationally. The company's properties feature a diverse range of key tenants, including department stores, specialty retailers, and dining and entertainment options. As one of the largest shopping mall operators in the world, Simon Property Group plays a significant role in shaping the retail landscape and driving economic growth in local communities.
