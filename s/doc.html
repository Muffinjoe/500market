About Healthpeak Properties

Healthpeak Properties is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests in and manages a diversified portfolio of healthcare properties, including medical offices, outpatient facilities, and senior living communities. The company's properties are primarily leased to healthcare providers and operators, generating rental income and providing a stable source of returns for investors. As a leading healthcare-focused REIT, Healthpeak Properties plays a critical role in supporting the delivery of healthcare services and providing a platform for investors to participate in the growing demand for healthcare real estate.
