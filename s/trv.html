About Travelers Companies (The)

The Travelers Companies, Inc. is a leading provider of property casualty insurance products and services to businesses, individuals, and institutions worldwide. The company offers a range of insurance products, including commercial property, casualty, and specialty insurance, as well as surety and fidelity bonds. As one of the largest property casualty insurance companies in the US, Travelers plays a significant role in protecting businesses and individuals from financial losses due to accidents, natural disasters, and other unforeseen events.
