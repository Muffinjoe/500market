About Costco

Costco Wholesale is a multinational American multinational retailer that operates a chain of membership-based warehouse clubs offering a wide selection of products, including fresh produce, meats, dairy products, electronics, clothing, and home goods. The company provides a one-stop shopping experience for its members, offering competitive prices on a vast array of products and services. As one of the world's largest retailers, Costco plays a significant role in the global consumer staples market, serving millions of members worldwide.
