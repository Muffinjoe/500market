About Hershey Company (The)

The Hershey Company is a leading American multinational consumer goods company that develops, manufactures, markets, and sells a wide range of chocolate and non-chocolate confectionery products, as well as snack foods and beverages. Key products include Hershey's Milk Chocolate bars, Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, and Kisses, among others. As one of the largest chocolate manufacturers in North America, the company plays a significant role in the global confectionery industry.
