About Lennox International

Lennox International is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and marketing of climate control, air quality, and air purification products. The company offers a range of products, including air conditioners, furnaces, heat pumps, and air purification systems, serving both residential and commercial markets. As a leading provider of climate control solutions, Lennox International plays a significant role in enhancing indoor air quality and comfort for consumers worldwide.
