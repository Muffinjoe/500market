About American Express

American Express is a global financial services company that provides a range of products and services, including charge and credit cards, travel services, and merchant payment processing solutions. The company's key products include its popular consumer and business credit cards, as well as its travel rewards programs and loyalty initiatives. As a leading provider of payment and travel services, American Express plays a significant role in facilitating global commerce and consumer spending.
