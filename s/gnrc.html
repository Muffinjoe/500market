About Generac

Generac Holdings Inc designs, manufactures, and sells power generation equipment and other power products for residential, commercial, industrial, and construction markets. The company's product portfolio includes portable and standby generators, home standby power systems, and other power solutions. As a leading provider of backup power systems, Generac plays a critical role in ensuring the reliability and resilience of critical infrastructure and emergency services.
