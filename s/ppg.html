About PPG Industries

PPG Industries is a multinational materials company that develops and delivers a range of coatings, specialty materials, and glass products for various industries, including aerospace, automotive, construction, and consumer electronics. The company's key products and services include paints, coatings, sealants, solar films, and architectural glass, among others. As a leading supplier of materials solutions, PPG Industries plays a critical role in enabling innovation and sustainability across multiple sectors.
