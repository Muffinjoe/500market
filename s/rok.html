About Rockwell Automation

Rockwell Automation is a leading industrial automation company that designs, builds, and supports industrial automation and information solutions, including hardware, software, and services, to help manufacturers optimize their production processes. The company's key products and services include programmable logic controllers, human-machine interfaces, and industrial software, as well as its FactoryTalk and FactorySphere platforms. By enabling industrial automation, Rockwell Automation helps manufacturers improve efficiency, productivity, and quality, making it a critical component of the global manufacturing ecosystem.
