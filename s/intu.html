About Intuit

Intuit is a technology company that provides business and financial management solutions to individuals and small businesses. Its key products and services include TurboTax, QuickBooks, and Mint, which enable users to manage their finances, prepare taxes, and run their businesses. By simplifying financial tasks and providing personalized insights, Intuit helps its customers achieve financial stability and success.
