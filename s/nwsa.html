About News Corp (Class A)

News Corp (Class A) is a global media and information services company that operates in the areas of news and information, book publishing, digital real estate services, and cable network programming. The company's key products and services include The Wall Street Journal, HarperCollins Publishers, and Foxtel, a pay-television service in Australia. As a leading global media company, News Corp plays a significant role in shaping public discourse and providing access to information and entertainment.
