About Netflix

Netflix is a global streaming service provider that offers a wide range of TV shows, movies, and documentaries to its subscribers. The company's key products include its flagship streaming service, which offers both ad-supported and ad-free plans, as well as its DVD rental service, which allows customers to rent DVDs by mail. As a leading player in the streaming industry, Netflix has disrupted traditional television and film distribution models, providing users with on-demand access to a vast library of content.
