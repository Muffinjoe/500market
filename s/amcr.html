About Amcor

Amcor is a global leader in the design and manufacture of packaging products for the food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and medical industries. The company's product portfolio includes flexible and rigid plastic packaging, glass and metal containers, and specialty cartons, serving a diverse range of customers worldwide. As a key player in the packaging industry, Amcor plays a critical role in ensuring the safety, quality, and sustainability of packaged goods.
