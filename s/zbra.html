About Zebra Technologies

Zebra Technologies is a global leader in enterprise asset tracking and automated identification solutions, offering a range of products and services that enable businesses to track, manage, and optimize their operations. The company's portfolio includes barcode scanners, printers, mobile computers, and software solutions that facilitate inventory management, supply chain visibility, and data analytics. By providing innovative technology solutions, Zebra Technologies helps organizations improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance customer experiences.
