About DaVita

DaVita Inc is a leading provider of kidney care services and products in the United States, offering dialysis and transplantation services to patients with chronic kidney disease. The company operates a network of freestanding dialysis centers, acute hospital services, and affiliated physician practices, providing care to approximately 210,000 patients. DaVita's services and products help patients with end-stage renal disease (ESRD) manage their condition and improve their quality of life.
