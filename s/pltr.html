About Palantir Technologies

Palantir Technologies is a software company that develops and sells data integration and analytics platforms to commercial and government clients. Its key products include Palantir Gotham, a data integration platform, and Palantir Foundry, a cloud-based data management platform. By providing advanced data management and analytics capabilities, Palantir helps organizations make data-driven decisions and gain insights from complex data sets.
