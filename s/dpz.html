About Domino's

Domino's Pizza, Inc. is a leading global pizza delivery company that operates a network of company-owned and franchise-owned stores. The company offers a variety of pizzas, sides, and desserts through its online ordering platform, mobile app, and in-store locations, catering to changing consumer preferences for convenience and digital ordering. As a prominent player in the quick-service restaurant industry, Domino's matters for its significant market share and influence on the consumer discretionary sector.
