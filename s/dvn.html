About Devon Energy

Devon Energy is an independent energy company that explores, develops, and produces oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids in the United States and Canada. The company's operations primarily focus on onshore oil and natural gas production, with a presence in the Permian Basin, the Delaware Basin, and other key shale plays. As a leading producer of oil and natural gas, Devon Energy plays a significant role in meeting global energy demand.
