About L3Harris

L3Harris Technologies is a leading global aerospace and defense technology company that provides a broad portfolio of communication, electronic warfare, navigation, and other mission-critical solutions for military, government, and commercial customers. The company's products and services include tactical communications, electronic warfare systems, navigation systems, and cybersecurity solutions. As a major player in the defense industry, L3Harris plays a critical role in supporting national security and global defense efforts.
