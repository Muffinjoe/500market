About Rollins, Inc.

Rollins, Inc. is a holding company that provides pest and termite control services through its subsidiaries, including Orkin, HomeFree Pest Control, and other regional brands. The company offers a range of services, including residential and commercial pest control, termite protection, and wildlife control, leveraging its extensive network of branches and technicians. As a leading provider of pest control services, Rollins, Inc. plays a significant role in maintaining public health and safety.
