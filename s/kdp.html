About Keurig Dr Pepper

Keurig Dr Pepper is a leading beverage company that operates in the United States and Canada, offering a diverse portfolio of hot and cold beverages, including coffee, tea, water, juice, and soda. The company's key brands include Keurig, Dr Pepper, 7 Up, Snapple, and A&W, among others. As a major player in the beverage industry, Keurig Dr Pepper matters to consumers and investors alike, providing a wide range of popular brands and innovative products.
