About O’Reilly Automotive

O'Reilly Automotive is a leading retailer of automotive aftermarket parts, tools, and supplies. The company operates a chain of stores offering a wide range of products, including new and remanufactured parts, tools, and accessories, as well as professional service provider offerings. As a key player in the automotive aftermarket industry, O'Reilly Automotive serves both do-it-yourself (DIY) and professional customers, providing essential products and services to support vehicle maintenance and repair.
