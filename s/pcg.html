About PG&E Corporation

PG&E Corporation is a holding company that operates through its principal subsidiary, Pacific Gas and Electric Company, providing electricity and natural gas to approximately 16 million customers in Northern and Central California. The company offers a range of products and services, including electricity and natural gas supply, transmission and distribution, and energy efficiency and renewable energy solutions. As one of the largest combined natural gas and electric utility companies in the United States, PG&E Corporation plays a critical role in meeting the energy needs of its customers and supporting the economic and environmental sustainability of the region.
