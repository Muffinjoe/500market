About American International Group

American International Group (AIG) is a leading global insurance organization providing a wide range of insurance products and services to individuals and businesses worldwide. The company's key products include property and casualty insurance, life insurance, and retirement savings products, as well as financial services and asset management. As one of the largest insurance companies globally, AIG plays a significant role in managing risk and providing financial security to its customers and stakeholders.
