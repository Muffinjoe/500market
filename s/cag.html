About Conagra Brands

Conagra Brands is a leading food company that produces a diverse portfolio of packaged food products for retail and foodservice customers. The company's key brands include Healthy Choice, Marie's, Reddi-wip, and Orville Redenbacher's, among others, offering a range of frozen, canned, and ready-to-eat meals, snacks, and condiments. As a major player in the consumer staples sector, Conagra Brands provides essential food products to households and businesses worldwide.
