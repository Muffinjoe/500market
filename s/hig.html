About Hartford (The)

The Hartford Financial Services Group, Inc. is a multinational insurance and financial services company that provides property and casualty insurance, group benefits, mortgage insurance, and investment products to individuals and businesses. The company's key products and services include auto, home, and workers' compensation insurance, as well as group life and disability insurance. As a leading insurance provider, The Hartford plays a significant role in protecting individuals and businesses from financial risks, making it a vital component of the global financial ecosystem.
