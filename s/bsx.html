About Boston Scientific

Boston Scientific is a medical technology company that develops, manufactures, and markets medical devices used in various specialties, including cardiology, endoscopy, urology, and neuromodulation. The company's product portfolio includes interventional cardiology products, such as stents and catheters, as well as minimally invasive surgical devices and implantable neurostimulators. Boston Scientific's innovative medical technologies help improve patient outcomes and quality of life, making it a leading player in the global healthcare industry.
