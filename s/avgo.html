About Broadcom

Broadcom Inc is a technology company that designs, develops, and supplies a range of semiconductor and infrastructure software solutions, including Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, storage, networking, and software management products. The company's products are used in various industries, including data center, enterprise, and mobile devices, enabling faster, more secure, and efficient data transfer and processing. As a leading provider of connectivity and infrastructure solutions, Broadcom plays a critical role in driving the development of modern technologies and transforming the way people live and work.
