About Molina Healthcare

Molina Healthcare is a health insurance company that provides managed care services to low-income individuals and families through government-sponsored programs, including Medicaid and the State Children's Health Insurance Program. The company offers a range of products and services, including health insurance plans, pharmacy benefits management, and care management programs. By providing access to affordable healthcare, Molina Healthcare plays a critical role in improving the health outcomes and well-being of vulnerable populations.
