About Align Technology

Align Technology is a medical device company that designs, manufactures, and markets a range of dental and orthodontic products, including the Invisalign clear aligner system and iTero intraoral scanners. These products enable orthodontists, dentists, and other healthcare professionals to provide patients with more efficient, effective, and aesthetically pleasing orthodontic and restorative treatments. By expanding access to high-quality orthodontic care, Align Technology is helping to transform the dental and orthodontic industries.
