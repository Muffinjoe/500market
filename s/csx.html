About CSX Corporation

CSX Corporation is a leading transportation company, providing rail-based freight transportation services to customers across a broad array of markets, including coal, agricultural and food products, construction materials, and intermodal. The company operates a network of approximately 21,000 miles of track and serves more than 70 ports and border crossings. As a critical component of the US supply chain, CSX plays a vital role in the movement of goods, supporting economic growth and development across the country.
