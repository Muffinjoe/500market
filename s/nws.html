About News Corp (Class B)

News Corp (Class B) is a global media and information services company that provides news, information, and content through a diverse portfolio of businesses, including news and media outlets, book publishing, and digital real estate services. The company's key products and services include The Wall Street Journal, New York Post, HarperCollins Publishers, and REA Group. As a leading player in the global media landscape, News Corp plays a significant role in shaping public discourse and providing essential information to consumers worldwide.
