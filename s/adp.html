About Automatic Data Processing

Automatic Data Processing (ADP) is a provider of human capital management (HCM) solutions to businesses of all sizes. The company offers a range of products and services, including payroll processing, benefits administration, and talent management software, to help organizations manage their workforce and improve operational efficiency. As a leading HCM solutions provider, ADP plays a critical role in supporting the global economy by enabling businesses to effectively manage their human resources and make informed decisions.
