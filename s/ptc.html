About PTC Inc.

PTC Inc. is a global provider of product lifecycle management (PLM), computer-aided design (CAD), and Internet of Things (IoT) solutions. The company offers a range of software products, including Creo, Windchill, and ThingWorx, which enable customers to design, develop, and operate complex products. By providing innovative technology solutions, PTC helps businesses improve product development, quality, and operational efficiency, driving innovation and growth in various industries.
