About General Mills

General Mills is a multinational food company that develops, manufactures, markets, and sells consumer foods worldwide. The company's portfolio includes well-known brands such as Cheerios, Betty Crocker, Pillsbury, and Yoplait, among others, offering a range of products including cereals, baking products, and frozen meals. As a leading player in the global food industry, General Mills matters for its significant presence in the consumer staples sector and its commitment to delivering high-quality, convenient food options to consumers worldwide.
