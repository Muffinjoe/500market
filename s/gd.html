About General Dynamics

General Dynamics is a leading aerospace and defense company that designs, builds, and delivers a wide range of products and services to the U.S. military, foreign governments, and commercial customers. Its key products and services include combat vehicles, ships, aircraft, and IT systems, as well as land and amphibious systems, and mission-critical communications and information technology solutions. As a major player in the defense industry, General Dynamics plays a critical role in supporting national security and global stability.
