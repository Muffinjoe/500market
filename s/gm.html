About General Motors

General Motors is a multinational corporation that designs, manufactures, and sells vehicles and vehicle parts worldwide. The company's key products include passenger cars, light trucks, and electric vehicles under various brands such as Chevrolet, Buick, GMC, and Cadillac. As one of the world's largest automakers, General Motors plays a significant role in shaping the global automotive industry and driving innovation in electric and autonomous vehicle technologies.
