About DTE Energy

DTE Energy is a diversified energy company that provides a range of energy-related services, including the generation, transmission, distribution, and sale of electricity and natural gas to residential, commercial, and industrial customers in Michigan. The company's key products and services include electricity, natural gas, and energy-related services such as energy efficiency and renewable energy solutions. As a leading energy provider in the Midwest, DTE Energy plays a critical role in meeting the region's energy needs and supporting economic growth and development.
