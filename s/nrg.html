About NRG Energy

NRG Energy is a leading integrated power company that generates, sells, and delivers energy and energy solutions to approximately 3.7 million residential, commercial, and industrial customers across the United States. The company operates a diverse portfolio of power generation assets, including natural gas, wind, and solar facilities, as well as a retail electricity business that offers a range of energy products and services. As one of the largest energy companies in the US, NRG Energy plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing energy demands while promoting a cleaner, more sustainable energy future.
