About Best Buy

Best Buy Co., Inc. operates as a leading multi-channel retailer of technology products and services in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. The company offers a wide range of products, including consumer electronics, home office products, appliances, and entertainment products, as well as services such as installation, maintenance, and repair. As a major player in the consumer electronics retail market, Best Buy provides customers with a one-stop shopping experience for their technology needs.
