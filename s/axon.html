About Axon Enterprise

Axon Enterprise is a technology company that develops and manufactures less-lethal weapons, body cameras, and software solutions for law enforcement, public safety, and military organizations. The company's key products include the Taser brand of stun guns, body cameras, and evidence management software, which enable agencies to collect, store, and analyze data. Axon Enterprise's technology helps improve public safety and reduce the use of force by law enforcement.
