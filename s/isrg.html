About Intuitive Surgical

Intuitive Surgical designs, manufactures, and markets robotic-assisted surgical systems for various medical procedures, including urology, gynecology, and general surgery. The company's flagship product, the da Vinci Surgical System, enables surgeons to perform complex procedures with enhanced precision and minimally invasive techniques. By improving patient outcomes and reducing recovery times, Intuitive Surgical's technology has become a standard in modern surgical care.
