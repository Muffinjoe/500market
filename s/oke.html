About Oneok

Oneok is a diversified energy company that operates through three business segments: natural gas gathering, processing, and transportation; natural gas liquids (NGLs) marketing; and natural gas and NGLs marketing. The company transports, stores, and markets natural gas and NGLs, providing critical infrastructure for the energy industry. As a major energy infrastructure provider, Oneok plays a vital role in connecting energy producers with consumers, supporting the efficient and reliable delivery of natural gas and NGLs.
