About Tapestry, Inc.

Tapestry, Inc. is a global house of fashion brands that designs, manufactures, and distributes a wide range of luxury and lifestyle products. The company's portfolio includes Coach, Kate Spade New York, and Stuart Weitzman, offering high-quality handbags, wallets, shoes, and other accessories. As a leading player in the global fashion industry, Tapestry, Inc. matters for its ability to drive consumer trends and deliver consistent growth through its iconic brands and strategic brand investments.
