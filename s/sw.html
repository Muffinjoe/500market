About Smurfit Westrock

Smurfit Kappa Group, trading as Smurfit Westrock on the NYSE under the ticker SW, is a multinational packaging company that manufactures and supplies paper-based packaging solutions, including corrugated containers, solid board, and other packaging materials. The company serves various industries, including food and beverage, e-commerce, and retail, providing innovative packaging solutions to meet the evolving needs of its customers. As a leading player in the packaging industry, Smurfit Westrock plays a critical role in reducing waste and promoting sustainable packaging practices.
