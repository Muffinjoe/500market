About Ecolab

Ecolab is a global leader in water, hygiene, and infection prevention solutions and services that protect people, brands, and the environment. The company provides a range of products and services, including cleaning and sanitizing solutions, water treatment and purification systems, and pest elimination services, primarily to the foodservice, hospitality, healthcare, and industrial markets. By helping customers reduce their environmental footprint and improve operational efficiency, Ecolab plays a critical role in promoting public health and sustainability.
