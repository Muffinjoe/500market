About Eaton Corporation

Eaton Corporation is a global diversified industrial manufacturer that provides solutions to help customers power, protect, and connect their equipment and vehicles. The company's key products and services include electrical systems, power management, hydraulics, and vehicle drivetrain and eMobility solutions. By enabling customers to improve efficiency, productivity, and safety, Eaton plays a critical role in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and renewable energy.
