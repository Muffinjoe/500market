About AES Corporation

AES Corp is a multinational energy company that generates and distributes electrical power to customers worldwide through a diverse portfolio of thermal and renewable energy sources. The company's key products and services include electricity generation, transmission, and distribution, as well as energy trading and marketing. As a leading player in the global energy sector, AES Corp plays a critical role in meeting the world's growing energy demands while promoting sustainable development and reducing carbon emissions.
