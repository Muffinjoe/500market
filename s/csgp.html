About CoStar Group

CoStar Group is a leading provider of commercial real estate information, analytics, and online marketplaces, offering a suite of products and services that enable clients to make informed property investment and leasing decisions. Its flagship platform, CoStar, provides access to a vast database of commercial properties, as well as market analytics and insights. By providing critical data and tools to the commercial real estate industry, CoStar Group helps facilitate more efficient and informed property transactions, driving growth and transparency in the market.
