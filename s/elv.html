About Elevance Health

Elevance Health is a health benefits company that provides a range of products and services, including health insurance, pharmacy benefits management, and care management, to individuals, employers, and government programs. The company's offerings include UnitedHealthcare, Optum, and other brands, providing access to healthcare services, pharmacy benefits, and data-driven insights to improve health outcomes. As one of the largest health insurance companies in the US, Elevance Health plays a critical role in shaping the nation's healthcare landscape.
