About Howmet Aerospace

Howmet Aerospace is a leading global supplier of advanced aluminum and titanium aerostructures, engine components, and other highly engineered products for the aerospace and defense industries. The company's products serve major commercial and military aircraft manufacturers, including Boeing and Lockheed Martin, and are critical components in the production of commercial airliners and military fighter jets. As a key supplier to the aerospace industry, Howmet Aerospace plays a vital role in the development and production of advanced aircraft and defense systems.
