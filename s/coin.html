About Coinbase

Coinbase is a digital currency exchange and financial services company that enables users to buy, sell, store, and trade cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others. The company's key products and services include its exchange platform, wallet, and custody solutions, as well as its institutional-grade trading and custody services for professional investors. As a leading player in the rapidly growing digital asset market, Coinbase plays a critical role in facilitating the adoption and mainstream acceptance of cryptocurrencies.
