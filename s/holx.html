About Hologic

Hologic is a medical technology company that develops, manufactures, and supplies diagnostic and medical imaging systems, as well as surgical and molecular diagnostic products. The company's key products include breast health and cervical cancer screening systems, molecular diagnostic instruments, and surgical products for gynecologic and urogynecologic procedures. By providing innovative solutions for women's health and cancer diagnosis, Hologic plays a crucial role in improving patient outcomes and advancing healthcare.
