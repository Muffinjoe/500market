About Corteva

Corteva, Inc. is a global agriculture company that develops and markets seeds, crop protection products, and digital solutions to help farmers improve crop yields and reduce environmental impact. The company's portfolio includes popular seed brands such as Pioneer and Brevant, as well as crop protection products like insecticides and herbicides. By providing innovative solutions to the agricultural industry, Corteva plays a critical role in meeting the world's growing demand for food and other agricultural products.
