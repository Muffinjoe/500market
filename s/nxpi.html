About NXP Semiconductors

NXP Semiconductors is a leading global semiconductor company that designs, develops, and manufactures high-performance, mixed-signal integrated circuits and other semiconductor solutions. The company's product portfolio includes automotive, industrial, mobile, and Internet of Things (IoT) solutions, as well as secure identification, automotive radar, and radio frequency (RF) products. NXP's innovative semiconductor solutions enable the development of advanced technologies that transform industries and improve people's lives.
