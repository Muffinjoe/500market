About Delta Air Lines

Delta Air Lines is a major American airline that operates a network of domestic and international routes, providing air transportation services to passengers and cargo. The company's key products and services include scheduled air transportation, cargo transportation, and loyalty programs. As one of the world's largest airlines, Delta plays a significant role in connecting people and businesses across the globe, driving economic growth and development.
