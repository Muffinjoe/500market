About Carvana

Carvana is an e-commerce platform that enables customers to purchase, finance, and service used vehicles online, offering a wide selection of vehicles and transparent pricing. The company's key products and services include its online marketplace, Carvana.com, as well as its network of physical vending machines and retail stores. By streamlining the car-buying process, Carvana has disrupted the traditional automotive retail industry and become a leading player in the used vehicle market.
