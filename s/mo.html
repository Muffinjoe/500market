About Altria

Altria Group, Inc. is a multinational consumer staples company that manufactures and sells tobacco products, including cigarettes, cigars, and smokeless tobacco. The company's portfolio includes iconic brands such as Marlboro, Virginia Slims, and Copenhagen, among others. As a leading player in the global tobacco industry, Altria's products and operations have a significant impact on the market and contribute to the company's position as a major player in the consumer staples sector.
