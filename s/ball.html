About Ball Corporation

Ball Corporation is a leading global supplier of innovative, sustainable aluminum packaging solutions for beverages, food, and household products. The company produces a wide range of products, including aluminum cans, bottles, and cups, as well as aerospace and other technologies. As a leading provider of sustainable packaging solutions, Ball Corporation plays a critical role in reducing waste and promoting environmental sustainability in the packaging industry.
