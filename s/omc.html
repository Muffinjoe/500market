About Omnicom Group

Omnicom Group is a global advertising, marketing, and corporate communications holding company that provides a range of services including advertising, public relations, and digital marketing. The company operates through a network of agencies and brands, offering services such as creative, media planning, and customer relationship management. As one of the largest advertising and marketing companies in the world, Omnicom Group plays a significant role in shaping the global marketing landscape.
