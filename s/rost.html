About Ross Stores

Ross Stores is a leading off-price apparel and home fashion retailer operating under the Ross Dress for Less and dd's DISCOUNTS banners. The company offers a wide selection of brand-name and designer merchandise at discounted prices, catering to value-conscious consumers. By providing affordable fashion options, Ross Stores has become a significant player in the retail industry, offering customers a unique shopping experience and driving sales growth through its efficient supply chain and merchandising strategies.
