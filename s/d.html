About Dominion Energy

Dominion Energy is a utility company that generates and distributes electricity and natural gas to approximately 7.5 million customers in 16 states. The company's key products and services include electricity and natural gas transmission, distribution, and sales, as well as regulated and non-regulated energy-related businesses. As a major energy provider in the United States, Dominion Energy plays a critical role in meeting the country's growing energy demands while also investing in renewable energy sources and grid modernization.
