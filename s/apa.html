About APA Corporation

APA Corporation is an independent oil and natural gas exploration and production company that operates onshore and offshore assets in the United States, Egypt, and the United Kingdom. The company's key products include crude oil, natural gas liquids, and natural gas, with a focus on developing and producing these resources in a safe and environmentally responsible manner. As a leading energy producer, APA Corporation plays a significant role in meeting global energy demand while navigating the transition to a lower-carbon future.
