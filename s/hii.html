About Huntington Ingalls Industries

Huntington Ingalls Industries is a leading American shipbuilding company that designs, builds, and maintains nuclear-powered aircraft carriers and submarines for the U.S. Navy, as well as commercial and government ships. The company's key products include nuclear-powered aircraft carriers, submarines, and other defense-related vessels, as well as non-nuclear ships and offshore energy facilities. As a major defense contractor, Huntington Ingalls Industries plays a critical role in supporting the U.S. military's national security and defense capabilities.
