About Entergy

Entergy Corporation is a diversified utility company that generates, transmits, and distributes electricity to approximately 2.9 million customers across the southern United States. The company's key products and services include nuclear, fossil fuel, and renewable energy generation, as well as transmission and distribution services. As one of the largest utilities in the country, Entergy plays a critical role in meeting the energy needs of its customers and contributing to the regional economy.
