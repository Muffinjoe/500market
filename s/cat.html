About Caterpillar Inc.

Caterpillar Inc is a leading manufacturer of heavy machinery and equipment, including earthmoving, mining, and construction equipment, as well as diesel and natural gas engines. The company's product portfolio includes wheel loaders, excavators, and motor graders, among others, which are used in various industries such as construction, mining, and energy. Caterpillar plays a significant role in global infrastructure development and is a key player in the industrials sector.
