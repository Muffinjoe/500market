About Campbell's Company (The)

Campbell's Company is a leading manufacturer and marketer of food and beverage products, offering a diverse portfolio of brands including Campbell's soups, Prego pasta sauces, Swanson broths, and V8 juices. The company's products are sold in over 120 countries and are a staple in many households, providing consumers with convenient and nutritious meal solutions. As a major player in the consumer staples industry, Campbell's Company plays a significant role in meeting the evolving needs of consumers for healthy, sustainable, and convenient food options.
