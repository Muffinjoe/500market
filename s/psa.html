About Public Storage

Public Storage is a real estate investment trust that owns and operates self-storage facilities across the United States. The company offers a range of storage solutions, including traditional storage units, climate-controlled storage, and vehicle storage, providing individuals and businesses with secure and convenient storage options. As one of the largest self-storage operators in the country, Public Storage plays a significant role in the storage industry, catering to the growing demand for flexible and accessible storage solutions.
