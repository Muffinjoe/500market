About Marriott International

Marriott International is a multinational hospitality company that operates, franchises, and licenses a portfolio of brands across the lodging industry, including luxury, select-service, and budget-friendly options. The company's key products and services include hotel management, franchise agreements, and loyalty programs, catering to a diverse range of travelers and guests. As a leading player in the global hospitality market, Marriott International matters for its significant impact on the travel and tourism industry.
