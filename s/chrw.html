About C.H. Robinson

C.H. Robinson is a global logistics and transportation company that provides a range of supply chain solutions to its customers, including freight brokerage, transportation management, and warehousing services. The company connects shippers with carriers through its network of over 300 offices worldwide, facilitating the movement of goods across various industries. By streamlining the logistics process, C.H. Robinson helps its customers reduce costs, improve efficiency, and enhance their overall supply chain resilience.
