About Schlumberger

SLB is a global energy services company that provides a range of products and services to the oil and natural gas industry, including drilling and completion, production, and project management solutions. The company's key products and services include drilling and completion fluids, cementing, and well intervention, as well as digital solutions for the energy industry. As a leading provider of energy services, SLB plays a critical role in helping to meet the world's increasing energy demands while also driving innovation and sustainability in the industry.
