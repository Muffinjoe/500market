About Teradyne

Teradyne is a leading provider of industrial automation and collaborative robots, offering a range of products and services that enable companies to improve efficiency and productivity in various industries. The company's key products include the Universal Robots brand of collaborative robots, as well as its own autonomous mobile robots and machine learning software. Teradyne's innovative solutions matter as they help manufacturers adapt to changing workforce dynamics and increasing demand for automation.
