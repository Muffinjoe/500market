About Chipotle Mexican Grill

Chipotle Mexican Grill is a fast-casual restaurant chain that specializes in Mexican-inspired food, offering burritos, bowls, tacos, salads, and other menu items made with responsibly sourced ingredients. The company operates over 2,000 locations globally, focusing on serving high-quality, sustainably sourced food to customers seeking a convenient and flavorful dining experience. As a leader in the fast-casual segment, Chipotle Mexican Grill matters to investors due to its strong brand recognition and growing demand for its unique offerings.
