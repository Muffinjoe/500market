About Progressive Corporation

Progressive Corporation is a leading provider of personal and commercial auto insurance, as well as other specialty property-casualty insurance products. The company offers a range of services, including auto insurance, home insurance, and motorcycle insurance, through its direct-to-consumer model and a network of independent agents. As one of the largest auto insurers in the US, Progressive plays a significant role in the country's insurance market.
