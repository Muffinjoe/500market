About Bio-Techne

Bio-Techne Corporation is a global life sciences company that develops, manufactures, and markets a range of biotechnology products and reagents used in research, diagnostics, and bioproduction. The company's key products include cytokines, growth factors, and other biochemical reagents, as well as instruments and consumables for life sciences research and diagnostics. Bio-Techne's products play a critical role in advancing medical research, diagnostics, and biotechnology applications, enabling scientists and researchers to better understand and combat diseases.
