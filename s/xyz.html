About Block, Inc.

Block, Inc. is a technology company that operates a suite of consumer and commercial payment products and services, including Cash App, a peer-to-peer payment platform, and Square, a point-of-sale and online payment processing system. The company's products enable individuals and businesses to manage their finances, accept payments, and access financial services. As a leading player in the digital payments space, Block, Inc. plays a significant role in shaping the future of money and commerce.
