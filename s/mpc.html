About Marathon Petroleum

Marathon Petroleum is a leading energy company that operates a nationwide portfolio of midstream, refining, and retail businesses. The company's key products and services include refining and marketing petroleum products, transporting crude oil and petroleum products, and operating a network of retail gasoline stations. As one of the largest refiners in the United States, Marathon Petroleum plays a critical role in meeting the country's energy demands.
