About Aon plc

Aon plc is a global professional services firm providing risk management, human resources, and retirement solutions to clients. The company's key products and services include insurance brokerage, reinsurance brokerage, and human capital consulting, helping clients navigate complex risks and optimize their benefits. As one of the largest insurance brokers worldwide, Aon plays a significant role in shaping the global risk landscape and advising clients on strategic business decisions.
