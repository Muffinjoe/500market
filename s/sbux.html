About Starbucks

Starbucks Corporation is a global coffee company that operates a chain of coffee shops, offering a range of specialty coffee beverages, teas, and other food and beverage items. The company's key products and services include its signature coffee drinks, as well as a selection of food items, including pastries, sandwiches, and salads. As a leading player in the coffee and retail industry, Starbucks matters to consumers and investors alike, with a strong brand presence and significant market share.
