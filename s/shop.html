About Shopify

Shopify is a Canadian e-commerce company that provides businesses with a platform to design, set up, and manage their online stores across various sales channels, including web, mobile, social media, online marketplaces, and physical retail locations. The company offers a suite of services, including payment processing, shipping and fulfillment, and customer engagement tools, to help merchants manage their online presence and grow their sales. As a leading e-commerce platform, Shopify enables businesses to reach a global customer base and compete in the rapidly evolving digital marketplace.
